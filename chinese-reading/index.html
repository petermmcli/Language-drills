▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Péngyǒu: Nǐ hǎo! Nǐ dào le ma? Wǒ zài jīchǎng děng nǐ. Xiǎohóng: Wǒ dào le! Wǒ gāngcái dào de, gāng xià fēijī. Péngyǒu: Nǐ zhècì zuò fēijī lái de, kuài ma? Xiǎohóng: Hěn kuài! Wǒ zuótiān mǎi de piào, hěn piányi. Péngyǒu: Tài hǎo le. Nǐ xiǎng qù nǎer wán? Xiǎohóng: Wǒ xiǎng qù gōngyuán, yě xiǎng qù cāntīng chī hǎochī de cài.
Friend: Hi! Have you arrived? I'm waiting for you at the airport. Xiaohong: I've arrived! I just got here — just got off the plane. Friend: You came by plane this time — was it fast? Harder 去北京旅游
去年，我坐飞机去北京旅游。🔊 那是我第一次坐飞机。🔊 我在北京学的中文，所以可以用中文聊天，非常开心。🔊 每天早上先去餐厅吃早饭，然后出去逛逛。🔊 吃完饭，去博物馆看看，或者去公园休息。🔊 北京的菜比我想的好吃多了，我最喜欢吃火锅。🔊 我觉得北京太有意思了，以后想再去一次。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Qùnián, wǒ zuò fēijī qù Běijīng lǚyóu. Nà shì wǒ dì yī cì zuò fēijī. Wǒ zài Běijīng xué de Zhōngwén, suǒyǐ kěyǐ yòng Zhōngwén liáotiān, fēicháng kāixīn. Měi tiān zǎoshang xiān qù cāntīng chī zǎofàn, ránhòu chūqù guàngguàng. Chī wán fàn, qù bówùguǎn kànkan, huòzhě qù gōngyuán xiūxi. Běijīng de cài bǐ wǒ xiǎng de hǎochī duō le, wǒ zuì xǐhuān chī huǒguō. Wǒ juéde Běijīng tài yǒuyìsi le, yǐhòu xiǎng zài qù yī cì.
Last year, I traveled to Beijing by plane. It was my first time on a plane. I learned Chinese in Beijing, so I could chat in Chinese — very happy. Every morning I would first go to a restaurant for breakfast, then head out to wander around. After meals, I'd go check out the museum, or go to the park to rest. Beijing's food was much more delicious than I expected — my favorite was hotpot. I think Beijing is incredibly interesting — I'd love to go again someday.
