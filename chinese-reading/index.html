Easy 上课的一天
今天早上，我坚地邽去学校上课。🔊 老师让大家只用中文说话，不可以说英语。🔊 我们已经练习了很多次，说得比以前好多了。🔊 下课以后，同学们都笑着说下次再见。🔊 我觉得学中文很有意思，也很有用。🔊
► Pinyin ► English
Jīn tiān zǎo shang, wǒ zuò dì tiě qù xué xiào shàng kè. Lǎo shī ràng dà jiā zhǐ yòng zhōng wén shuō huà, bù kě yǐ shuō yīng yǔ. Wǒ men yǐ jīng liàn xí le hěn duō cì, shuō de bǐ yǐ qián hǎo duō le. Xià kè yǐ hòu, tóng xué men dōu xiào zhe shuō xià cì zài jiàn. Wǒ jué de xué zhōng wén hěn yǒu yì si, yě hěn yǒu yòng.
This morning, I took the subway to school for class. The teacher had everyone only speak in Chinese — no English allowed. We have already practiced many times and speak much better than before. After class, everyone laughed and said "see you next time." I think studying Chinese is very interesting and also very useful.

Harder 看吏生
前天，我开始生病了，身体不太好。🔊 妈妈问我为什么不去看吏生。🔊 我说我正在喳尚茶，可能不用去。🔊 栄天早上，我已经连说话都很难了。🔊 所以，妈妈带我去了医院。🔊 医生让我多休息，还给了我一些说。🔊 虽然说有点荇，我每天都吃。🔊 医生说后天就会好多了。🔊 生病以后，我觉得健康比什么都重要。🔊
► Pinyin ► English
Qián tiān, wǒ kāi shǐ shēng bìng le, shēn tǐ bú tài hǎo. Māma wèn wǒ wèi shén me bú qù kàn yī shēng. Wǒ shuō wǒ zhèng zài hē jiāng chá, kě néng bú yòng qù. Zuó tiān zǎo shang, wǒ yǐ jīng lián shuō huà dōu hěn nán le. Suǒ yǐ, mā ma dài wǒ qù le yī yuàn. Yī shēng ràng wǒ duō xiū xi, hái gěi le wǒ yì xiē yào. Suī rán yào yǒu diǎn kǔ, wǒ měi tiān dōu chī. Yī shēng shuō hòu tiān jiù huì hǎo duō le. Shēng bìng yǐ hòu, wǒ jué de jiàn kāng bǐ shén me dōu zhòng yào.
The day before yesterday, I started getting sick — not feeling well at all. Mom asked me why I wasn't going to see a doctor. I said I was drinking ginger tea and probably didn't need to go. But yesterday morning, I could barely even speak anymore. So Mom took me to the hospital. The doctor told me to rest more and gave me some medicine. Even though the medicine tastes a little bitter, I take it every day. The doctor said I'll be much better the day after tomorrow. After getting sick, I feel that health is more important than anything.
