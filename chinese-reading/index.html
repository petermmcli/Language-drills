Easy 我家附近
我家附近有一个公园，公园对面是一家餐厅。🔊 餐厅旁边有一家咖啡店，很方便。🔊 我经常去那家咖啡店喝咖啡。🔊 有时候，我也去公园休息，公园里有很多树，很漂亮。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Wǒ jiā fù jìn yǒu yī gè gōng yuán， gōng yuán duì miàn shì yī jiā cān tīng。 Cān tīng páng biān yǒu yī jiā kā fēi diàn， hěn fāng biàn。 Wǒ jīng cháng qù nà jiā kā fēi diàn hē kā fēi。 Yǒu shí hou， wǒ yě qù gōng yuán xiū xi， gōng yuán lǐ yǒu hěn duō shù， hěn piào liang。
Near my home there is a park, and across from the park is a restaurant. Next to the restaurant there is a coffee shop — very convenient. I often go to that coffee shop to drink coffee. Sometimes I also go to the park to rest; the park has many trees and is very beautiful.

A: Excuse me, where is the school? B: The school is right across from the bank — only five minutes on foot. A: Thank you! What is in front of the bank? B: In front of the bank there is a park, and inside there are many trees. A: Wonderful! I would like to go rest in the park. B: Sure! The park is very beautiful — you will like it.

Harder 我的好朋友
我有一个好朋友，他很喜欢给我举例子。🔊 他告诉我，学习汉语很有意思，也很重要。🔊 有时候，他站在我面前说，早上学习比下午好。🔊 他还给我举了一个例子：每天早上学一个小时汉字。🔊 他说的话很有道理，我也觉得很合理。🔊 他家就在我学校对面，所以我们经常一起去公园休息。🔊 我觉得有这样的好朋友，非常开心。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Wǒ yǒu yī gè hǎo péng yǒu， tā hěn xǐ huān gěi wǒ jǔ lì zi。 Tā gào su wǒ， xué xí Hàn yǔ hěn yǒu yì si， yě hěn zhòng yào。 Yǒu shí hou， tā zhàn zài wǒ miàn qián shuō， zǎo shàng xué xí bǐ xià wǔ hǎo。 Tā hái gěi wǒ jǔ le yī gè lì zi： měi tiān zǎo shàng xué yī gè xiǎo shí Hàn zì。 Tā shuō de huà hěn yǒu dào lǐ， wǒ yě jué de hěn hé lǐ。 Tā jiā jiù zài wǒ xué xiào duì miàn， suǒ yǐ wǒ men jīng cháng yī qǐ qù gōng yuán xiū xi。 Wǒ jué de yǒu zhè yàng de hǎo péng yǒu， fēi cháng kāi xīn。
I have a good friend who loves giving me examples. He told me that studying Chinese is very interesting and also very important. Sometimes he stands in front of me and says that studying in the morning is better than in the afternoon. He also gave me an example: study Chinese characters for one hour every morning. What he says makes a lot of sense, and I also think it is very reasonable. His home is right across from my school, so we often go to the park together to rest. I feel that having such a good friend makes me very happy.
