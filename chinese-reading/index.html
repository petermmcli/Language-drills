Easy 咖啡店
今天下午，我和朋友进了一家咖啡店。🔊 我点了一杯拿铁，她点了另一个。🔊 她说她的咖啡很有意思，甜甜的。🔊 我也喝了一些，真的比我的拿铁好喝。🔊 我们坐在咖啡店里聊天，很开心。🔊

This afternoon, my friend and I went into a coffee shop. I ordered a latte, and she ordered the other one. She said her coffee was very interesting — sweet. I had a little too, and it really tasted better than my latte. We sat in the coffee shop chatting, very happy.

Mèimei: Nǐ de qián gòu ma? Gēge: Gòu, dànshì wǒ juéde mǎi piányi de gèng hélǐ. Mèimei: Ng, nǐ shuō de yǒu dàolǐ. Piányi de yě hěn yǒuyìsi.
Older brother: I want to buy a new phone, but I've seen two and don't know which one to pick. Younger sister: Which two phones? Older brother: One is cheap but not new. The other is very new but not cheap. Younger sister: Do you have enough money? Older brother: Enough, but I think buying the cheaper one is more reasonable. Younger sister: Mm, what you say makes sense. The cheap one is also pretty interesting.

Harder 星期天的博物馆
星期天早上，我和朋友一起进了博物馆。🔊 我们先看了一些非常有意思的东西，然后去了另一个地方。🔊 我朋友说她最喜欢这个博物馆，因为里面有很多新的东西。🔊 我觉得她说得很有道理，看东西的时候时间过得快多了。🔊 看完博物馆，我们一起吃了一些点心，又去了星巴克。🔊 我跟她说和朋友一起做事比自己一个人合理多了。🔊 她也同意，下次还要再一起出去。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Xīngqītiān zǎoshang, wǒ hé péngyǒu yìqǐ jìn le bówùguǎn. Wǒmen xiān kàn le yìxiē fēicháng yǒuyìsi de dōngxi, ránhòu qù le lìng yí ge dìfang. Wǒ péngyǒu shuō tā zuì xǐhuān zhè ge bówùguǎn, yīnwèi lǐmiàn yǒu hěn duō xīn de dōngxi. Wǒ juéde tā shuō de hěn yǒu dàolǐ, kàn dōngxi de shíhou shíjiān guò de kuài duō le. Kàn wán bówùguǎn, wǒmen yìqǐ chī le yìxiē diǎnxīn, yòu qù le Xīngbākè. Wǒ gēn tā shuō hé péngyǒu yìqǐ zuòshì bǐ zìjǐ yí ge rén hélǐ duō le. Tā yě tóngyì, xiàcì hái yào zài yìqǐ chūqù.
Sunday morning, my friend and I went into the museum together. We first looked at some very interesting things, and then went to another place. My friend said she likes this museum the most, because inside there are many new things. I think what she said makes a lot of sense — when you're looking at things, time flies. After finishing at the museum, we ate some dim sum together, then went to Starbucks. I told her that doing things with a friend is much more sensible than doing them alone. She agreed too — next time we'll go out together again.
