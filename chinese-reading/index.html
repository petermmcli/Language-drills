Easy 吃烧卖
今天我和朋友一起吃烧卖。🔊 我们要了一些烧卖，非常好吃。🔊 我吃了一个就够了。🔊 但是我朋友又开心又喜欢吃烧卖。🔊 我记得以后还要再去那家餐厅。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Jīn tiān wǒ hé péng yǒu yì qǐ chī shāo mài . Wǒ men yào le yì xiē shāo mài , fēi cháng hǎo chī . Wǒ chī le yí ge jiù gòu le . Dàn shì wǒ péng yǒu yòu kāi xīn yòu xǐ huān chī shāo mài . Wǒ jì de yǐ hòu hái yào zài qù nà jiā cān tīng .
Today my friend and I ate shumai together. We ordered some shumai, and they were very delicious. I had only one and that was enough. But my friend was happy and loved eating shumai. I remember we want to go back to that restaurant later.

Medium 新的城市
哥哥：你的假期怎么样？去哪儿了？🔊 妹妹：我又去了一个新的城市，叫北京。🔊 哥哥：北京怎么样？🔊 妹妹：北京有很多店，颜色都很漂亮。🔊 哥哥：你吃了什么？🔊 妹妹：我吃了烧卖，非常好吃！🔊 哥哥：以后还要再去吗？🔊 妹妹：看情况吧。下次见以后，我又要去一个新的城市，因为我开心。🔊

Mèi mei: Běi jīng yǒu hěn duō diàn , yán sè dōu hěn piào liang . Gē ge: Nǐ chī le shén me ? Mèi mei: Wǒ chī le shāo mài , fēi cháng hǎo chī ! Gē ge: Yǐ hòu hái yào zài qù ma ? Mèi mei: Kàn qíng kuàng ba . Xià cì jiàn yǐ hòu , wǒ yòu yào qù yí ge xīn de chéng shì , yīn wèi wǒ kāi xīn .
Older brother: How was your vacation? Where did you go? Younger sister: I went to a new city again — Beijing. Older brother: How was Beijing? Younger sister: Beijing has lots of shops, and the colors are all beautiful. Older brother: What did you eat? Younger sister: I had shumai — really delicious! Older brother: Will you go again later? Younger sister: It depends. After we meet next time, I'll go to another new city again, because I'm happy.

Harder 朋友的城市
上个星期，我和朋友去了一个新的城市。🔊 城市非常大，我朋友的房子有三层。🔊 我们坐在一家餐厅里吃烧卖，又开心又好。🔊 我朋友是一个非常可靠的人。🔊 她告诉我："以后我还要再去那个城市。"🔊 我说："看情况吧。"🔊 我朋友说："下次见以后，你可以在Chat里发给我一些新词。"🔊 老样子，我都知道，我又记得又开心。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Shàng ge xīng qī , wǒ hé péng yǒu qù le yí ge xīn de chéng shì . Chéng shì fēi cháng dà , wǒ péng yǒu de fáng zi yǒu sān céng . Wǒ men zuò zài yì jiā cān tīng lǐ chī shāo mài , yòu kāi xīn yòu hǎo . Wǒ péng yǒu shì yí ge fēi cháng kě kào de rén . Tā gào sù wǒ : " Yǐ hòu wǒ hái yào zài qù nà ge chéng shì . " Wǒ shuō : " Kàn qíng kuàng ba . " Wǒ péng yǒu shuō : " Xià cì jiàn yǐ hòu , nǐ kě yǐ zài Chat lǐ fā gěi wǒ yì xiē xīn cí . " Lǎo yàng zi , wǒ dōu zhī dào , wǒ yòu jì de yòu kāi xīn .
Last week, my friend and I went to a new city. The city was very big — my friend's house has three floors. We sat in a restaurant eating shumai — happy and content. My friend is a very reliable person. She told me: "I want to go back to that city later." I said: "It depends." My friend said: "Next time we meet, you can send me some new words in Chat." Same as always — I knew it all, both remembering and happy.
