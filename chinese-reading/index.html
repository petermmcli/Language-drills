Harder 在家做饭
今天天气很热，我开着空调在家做饭。🔊 先出门去超市，买了一些有机橙子和蔬菜。🔊 回来以后，妈妈告诉我先洗蔬菜，然后再做主食。🔊 妹妹穿着新衬衫来帮忙，家里的风扇也开着。🔊 和以前比，今天的菜好吃多了，大家都很开心。🔊 吃完饭，妹妹拿手机录音，把今天的声音都录下来了。🔊 我觉得在家做饭比去饭馆好多了，以后我们要经常在家做。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Jīntiān tiānqì hěn rè，wǒ kāizhe kōngtiáo zài jiā zuòfàn。 Xiān chūmén qù chāoshì，mǎi le yìxiē yǒujī chéngzi hé shūcài。 Huílái yǐhòu，māma gàosu wǒ xiān xǐ shūcài，ránhòu zài zuò zhǔshí。 Mèimei chuānzhe xīn chènshān lái bāngmáng，jiā lǐ de fēngshān yě kāizhe。 Hé yǐqián bǐ，jīntiān de cài hǎochī duō le，dàjiā dōu hěn kāixīn。 Chīwán fàn，mèimei ná shǒujī lùyīn，bǎ jīntiān de shēngyīn dōu lù xiàlái le。 Wǒ juéde zài jiā zuòfàn bǐ qù fànguǎn hǎo duō le，yǐhòu wǒmen yào jīngcháng zài jiā zuò。
It's very hot today — I'm cooking at home with the air conditioning on. First I went out to the supermarket and bought some organic oranges and vegetables. After coming back, Mom told me to wash the vegetables first, then cook the staple food. My younger sister came to help wearing a new shirt, and the electric fan at home was also on. Compared to before, today's food tasted much better — everyone was very happy. After dinner, my sister took her phone and made a recording, capturing all the sounds from today. I think cooking at home is much better than going to a restaurant — we should do it often from now on.
