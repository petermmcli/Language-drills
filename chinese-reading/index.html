Harder 坐车去开会
昨天早上，我要去公司开会。🔊 我先坐地铁，然后换公共汽车。🔊 车里人很多，不方便说话，所以我给同事发了信息，告诉他我快到了。🔊 坐了三十分钟以后，我到了公司。🔊 开完会以后，大家一起去餐厅吃饭聊天。🔊 我觉得和同事说话非常开心，比工作有趣多了。🔊 回家的时候，坐公共汽车比坐出租车便宜，也更快。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Zuótiān zǎoshàng, wǒ yào qù gōngsī kāi huì. Wǒ xiān zuò dìtiě, ránhòu huàn gōnggòng qìchē. Chē lǐ rén hěn duō, bù fāngbiàn shuōhuà, suǒyǐ wǒ gěi tóngshì fā le xìnxi, gàosu tā wǒ kuài dào le. Zuò le sānshí fēnzhōng yǐhòu, wǒ dào le gōngsī. Kāi wán huì yǐhòu, dàjiā yìqǐ qù cāntīng chīfàn liáotiān. Wǒ juéde hé tóngshì shuōhuà fēicháng kāixīn, bǐ gōngzuò yǒuqù duō le. Huí jiā de shíhou, zuò gōnggòng qìchē bǐ zuò chūzūchē piányi, yě gèng kuài.
Yesterday morning, I needed to go to the office for a meeting. I took the subway first, then transferred to a bus. The bus was crowded and it wasn't easy to chat, so I texted a colleague to say I was almost there. After riding for thirty minutes, I arrived at the office. After the meeting, everyone went to a restaurant together to eat and chat. I felt that chatting with colleagues was really fun — much more interesting than working. On the way home, taking the bus was cheaper than a taxi, and also faster.
