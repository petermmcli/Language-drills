I love sports most of all. Every Sunday morning I play football. Sometimes I also play basketball with friends. After playing ball, we often go to a restaurant for Chinese food. When I get home in the evening, I also like reading.

Harder 小红的周末
小红每个周末都做很多事。🔊 她先踢足球，然后骑车去商店。🔊 她觉得踢足球比打篮球更有趣。🔊 商店旁边有一个书店，她喜欢进去读书。🔊 她告诉朋友，她最喜欢的运动是攀岩，因为很难，但是非常有意思。🔊 朋友说，听说读写都很重要，运动也很重要。🔊 小红同意：运动和学习一样重要。🔊 以后，她希望每天踢足球，打篮球，还有攀岩。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Xiǎo hóng měi gè zhōu mò dōu zuò hěn duō shì. Tā xiān tī zú qiú, rán hòu qí chē qù shāng diàn. Tā jué de tī zú qiú bǐ dǎ lán qiú gèng yǒu qù. Shāng diàn páng biān yǒu yí gè shū diàn, tā xǐ huān jìn qù dú shū. Tā gào sù péng yǒu, tā zuì xǐ huān de yùn dòng shì pān yán, yīn wèi hěn nán, dàn shì fēi cháng yǒu yì si. Péng yǒu shuō, tīng shuō dú xiě dōu hěn zhòng yào, yùn dòng yě hěn zhòng yào. Xiǎo hóng tóng yì: yùn dòng hé xué xí yí yàng zhòng yào. Yǐ hòu, tā xī wàng měi tiān tī zú qiú, dǎ lán qiú, hái yǒu pān yán.
Xiaohong does many things every weekend. She plays football first, then cycles to the shop. She thinks playing football is more interesting than playing basketball. Next to the shop there is a bookstore, and she likes to go in and read. She told her friends that her favourite sport is rock climbing, because it is hard but very interesting. Her friend said that listening, speaking, reading, and writing are all important, and so is sport. Xiaohong agreed: sport and studying are equally important. In the future, she hopes to play football every day, play basketball, and also rock climb.
