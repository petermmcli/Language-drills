Easy 妹妹的周末
我的妹妹今年八岁，她很可爱。🔊 今天下午，妈妈去了银行，爸爸也去了公司。🔊 我问妹妹："你想去哪儿？"🔊 她说："我想去公园！好呀！"🔊 我们一起走去公园，非常开心。🔊
► Pinyin ► English
Wǒ de mèimei jīnnián bā suì, tā hěn kěài. Jīntiān xiàwǔ, māma qù le yínháng, bàba yě qù le gōngsī. Wǒ wèn mèimei: "Nǐ xiǎng qù nǎr?" Tā shuō: "Wǒ xiǎng qù gōngyuán! Hǎo ya!" Wǒmen yīqǐ zǒu qù gōngyuán, fēicháng kāixīn.
My younger sister is eight years old this year — she's very cute. This afternoon, Mom went to the bank and Dad also went to the office. I asked my sister: "Where do you want to go?" She said: "I want to go to the park! Great!" We walked to the park together and had a great time.

Medium 周末去哪儿了
哥哥：周末你去了哪儿？🔊 妹妹：我去了书店，买了很多书。你呢？🔊 哥哥：我和爸爸妈妈去了公园。妈妈今年五十岁，很开心！🔊 妹妹：好呀！听说那个公园很有名。🔊 哥哥：对！妈妈说以后每个周末都要去。🔊 妹妹：那下次可以带我去吗？🔊
► Pinyin ► English
Gēge: Zhōumò nǐ qù le nǎr? Mèimei: Wǒ qù le shūdiàn, mǎi le hěn duō shū. Nǐ ne? Gēge: Wǒ hé bàba māma qù le gōngyuán. Māma jīnnián wǔshí suì, hěn kāixīn! Mèimei: Hǎo ya! Tīngshuō nà ge gōngyuán hěn yǒumíng. Gēge: Duì! Māma shuō yǐhòu měi ge zhōumò dōu yào qù. Mèimei: Nà xiàcì kěyǐ dài wǒ qù ma?
Brother: Where did you go this weekend? Sister: I went to the bookstore and bought a lot of books. How about you? Brother: I went to the park with Mom and Dad. Mom turned fifty this year — so happy! Sister: Oh great! I've heard that park is really well-known. Brother: Yes! Mom says she wants to go every weekend from now on. Sister: Can you take me next time?

Harder 找礼物
朋友的爸爸今年六十岁，我们想给他买礼物。🔊 上个周末，我们先去了书店，看完了很多书，但是都觉得不好。🔊 然后去了商场，朋友买到了一个手表，比书好看多了！🔊 她说："好呀！爸爸一定很喜欢！"🔊 朋友有车，比走路快多了，很快到了她家。🔊 吃完饭，朋友给了爸爸手表。🔊 爸爸非常高兴，觉得这是最好的礼物。🔊
► Pinyin ► English
Péngyǒu de bàba jīnnián liùshí suì, wǒmen xiǎng gěi tā mǎi lǐwù. Shàng ge zhōumò, wǒmen xiān qù le shūdiàn, kàn wán le hěn duō shū, dànshì dōu juéde bù hǎo. Ránhòu qù le shāngchǎng, péngyǒu mǎi dào le yī ge shǒubiǎo, bǐ shū hǎokàn duō le! Tā shuō: "Hǎo ya! Bàba yídìng hěn xǐhuān!" Péngyǒu yǒu chē, bǐ zǒulù kuài duō le, hěn kuài dào le tā jiā. Chī wán fàn, péngyǒu gěi le bàba shǒubiǎo. Bàba fēicháng gāoxìng, juéde zhè shì zuì hǎo de lǐwù.
My friend's father is turning sixty this year, so we wanted to buy him a gift. Last weekend, we first went to the bookstore and finished looking through many books, but none seemed right. Then we went to the mall, and my friend found a watch — much nicer than any book! She said: "Great! Dad is sure to love it!" My friend has a car, much faster than walking, and we got to her house quickly. After dinner, my friend gave her dad the watch. Dad was overjoyed and said he felt it was the best gift he'd ever received.
