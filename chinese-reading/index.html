Easy 送礼物
妈妈的生日快到了。🔊 我想送给她一件漂亮的衣服。🔊 妹妹说，送她一本书也很好。🔊 我觉得这也是个好主意。🔊 我们一起去商场买了礼物。🔊 妈妈看到礼物，非常高兴。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Māma de shēngrì kuài dào le. Wǒ xiǎng sòng gěi tā yí jiàn piàoliàng de yīfu. Mèimei shuō, sòng tā yì běn shū yě hěn hǎo. Wǒ juéde zhè yě shì ge hǎo zhǔyi. Wǒmen yīqǐ qù shāngchǎng mǎi le lǐwù. Māma kàndào lǐwù, fēicháng gāoxìng.
Mom's birthday is coming up. I want to give her a beautiful piece of clothing. My younger sister said giving her a book would also be nice. I thought that was a good idea too. We went to the mall together and bought gifts. When Mom saw the gifts, she was overjoyed.

Xiǎohóng: Wǒ yào yī bēi nǎichá, bú tài tián de. Xiǎomíng: Hǎo, wǒ yě yào yī bēi. Chī wán fàn, wǒmen qù guàng shāngchǎng ba. Xiǎohóng: Hǎo zhǔyi! Wǒmen yǐjīng hǎo jiǔ méi qù le.

Harder 找房子
我最近想换一个新房子。🔊 上个星期，我先在网上找了一些地方，然后去看了两个房间。🔊 第一个房间在地铁站附近，很方便，但是太贵了。🔊 第二个房间比第一个便宜多了，房间里有床和桌子，我觉得很不错。🔊 看完了两个房间以后，我决定住第二个。🔊 妈妈说这是个好主意。🔊 住进去以后，我想买一些新东西送给自己，因为这是我第一次一个人住。🔊
▸ Pinyin ▸ English
Wǒ zuìjìn xiǎng huàn yī ge xīn fángzi. Shàng ge xīngqī, wǒ xiān zài wǎng shàng zhǎo le yīxiē dìfang, ránhòu qù kàn le liǎng ge fángjiān. Dì yī ge fángjiān zài dìtiě zhàn fùjìn, hěn fāngbiàn, dànshì tài guì le. Dì èr ge fángjiān bǐ dì yī ge piányi duō le, fángjiān lǐ yǒu chuáng hé zhuōzi, wǒ juéde hěn búcuò. Kàn wán le liǎng ge fángjiān yǐhòu, wǒ juédìng zhù dì èr ge. Māma shuō zhè shì ge hǎo zhǔyi. Zhù jìn qù yǐhòu, wǒ xiǎng mǎi yīxiē xīn dōngxi sòng gěi zìjǐ, yīnwèi zhè shì wǒ dì yī cì yī ge rén zhù.
I've been thinking about moving to a new place lately. Last week, I first looked online for some options, then went to see two apartments. The first room was near the subway station — very convenient, but too expensive. The second room was much cheaper than the first, had a bed and a desk inside, and I thought it was quite good. After viewing both rooms, I decided to move into the second one. Mom said it was a good idea. Once I move in, I want to buy a few new things as a gift to myself, because this is my first time living alone.

Comprehension Questions

▸ Question Pinyin
  1. 他最近想做什么？ ▸ answer → 他想换一个新房子。
  2. 第一个房间有什么问题？ ▸ answer → 太贵了。
  3. 第二个房间怎么样？ ▸ answer → 比第一个便宜多了，有床和桌子，很不错。
  4. 他决定住哪个房间？ ▸ answer → 他决定住第二个。
  5. 他为什么要买新东西送给自己？ ▸ answer → 因为这是他第一次一个人住。
1. Tā zuìjìn xiǎng zuò shénme? → Tā xiǎng huàn yī ge xīn fángzi.
2. Dì yī ge fángjiān yǒu shénme wèntí? → Tài guì le.
3. Dì èr ge fángjiān zěnmeyàng? → Bǐ dì yī ge piányi duō le, yǒu chuáng hé zhuōzi, hěn búcuò.
4. Tā juédìng zhù nǎ ge fángjiān? → Tā juédìng zhù dì èr ge.
5. Tā wèishénme yào mǎi xīn dōngxi sòng gěi zìjǐ? → Yīnwèi zhè shì tā dì yī cì yī ge rén zhù.
