Grammar

2. 越 + Adj/Verb, 越 + Adj/Verb — "The more X, the more Y" (review)

Expresses a proportional relationship. Structure: (Subject) 越 + Adj/Verb₁, (Subject) 越 + Adj/Verb₂. Compare with 越来越 + adj (getting more and more…), which describes change over time rather than linking two variables.

压力越大越好。🔊
Yālì yuè dà yuè hǎo.
The greater the pressure, the better.
我越学中文越觉得有意思。🔊
Wǒ yuè xué Zhōngwén yuè juéde yǒu yìsi.
The more I study Chinese, the more interesting I find it.
天气越来越冷了。🔊
Tiānqì yuèláiyuè lěng le.
The weather is getting colder and colder.
