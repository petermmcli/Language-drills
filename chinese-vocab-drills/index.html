Section 3: Fill in the Blank

Choose the word that best completes the sentence. Use 🔊 to hear the full sentence with the correct answer filled in.

1. 好的，______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Hǎo de，______。
► Answer
C. 下午见 — 好的，下午见。
Hǎo de，xiàwǔ jiàn。
OK, see you this afternoon. — 下午见 is a set farewell. 下午 = afternoon, 见 = to see. Contrast: 一会儿见 = see you in a moment; 再见 = general goodbye.

2. 我们的______很好。 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒmen de ______ hěn hǎo。
► Answer
A. 老师 — 我们的老师很好。
Wǒmen de lǎoshī hěn hǎo。
Our teacher is very nice. — 老师 = teacher. 同事 (colleague) and 朋友 (friend) are plausible but don’t match the school/classroom context.

3. 他明天可以______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā míngtiān kěyǐ ______。
► Answer
B. 出院 — 他明天可以出院。
Tā míngtiān kěyǐ chūyuàn。
He can leave the hospital tomorrow. — 出院 = to be discharged from hospital. 出 = exit, 院 = institution. Opposite: 住院 (zhùyuàn) = to be hospitalized.

4. 明天可能是______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Míngtiān kěnéng shì ______。
► Answer
C. 阴天 — 明天可能是阴天。
Míngtiān kěnéng shì yīntiān。
Tomorrow might be cloudy. — 阴天 = overcast/cloudy day. Contrast: 晴天 = sunny day. 每天 = every day — not a weather word, a common distractor.

5. 现在差五______八点。 🔊

► Pinyin
Xiànzài chā wǔ ______ bā diǎn。
► Answer
A. 分 — 现在差五分八点。
Xiànzài chā wǔ fēn bā diǎn。
It’s five minutes to eight now. — 差 (chā) = lacking/short of. 差五分八点 = five minutes short of eight = 7:55. 刻 = quarter-hour, 小时 = hour.
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: "I study Chinese every day."

► Answer
我每天都学中文。 🔊
Wǒ měitiān dōu xué Zhōngwén。
I study Chinese every day.
都 (dōu = all/every) often follows 每天 to reinforce regularity: 我每天都… = Every day I… Without 都 the sentence is still correct but less emphatic.

2. Translate: "Turn left when you reach the traffic light."

► Answer
到红绿灯那儿就左转。 🔊
Dào hónglüdēng nàr jiù zuǒzhuǎn。
Turn left when you reach the traffic light.
到…那儿 = when you reach… 就 here means then (as soon as you get there). 左转 = turn left; 右转 = turn right.

3. Translate: "I like white clothes."

► Answer
我喜欢白色的衣服。 🔊
Wǒ xǐhuan báisè de yīfu。
I like white clothes.
Color adjectives pair with 色 (sè) before nouns: 白色的衣服 = white clothes. 的 links the color to the noun.

4. Translate: "There are a lot of people today."

► Answer
今天有好多人。 🔊
Jīntiān yǒu hǎoduō rén。
There are a lot of people today.
好多 is colloquial for a lot of / quite many. More formal: 很多. 有好多… = there are a lot of…

5. Translate: "What is the answer to this question?"

► Answer
这个问题的答案是什么？ 🔊
Zhège wèntí de dáàn shì shénme？
What is the answer to this question?
答案 is a noun (the answer/solution). Don’t confuse with 回答 (huí dá) = to answer/reply (verb).
