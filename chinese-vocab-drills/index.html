Section 1: Chinese → English

1. 城市 🔊

► Pinyin
chéng shì
► Answer
B. city — 城市 (chéng shì) = city. Don't confuse with 市场 (shì chǎ) = market/shopping area.

2. 考试 🔊

► Pinyin
kǎo shì
► Answer
A. exam / test — 考试 (kǎo shì). 考 means to test/examine, 试 means to try/test. Together = a formal exam.

3. 坐飞机 🔊

► Pinyin
zuò fēi jī
► Answer
B. take a plane / fly — 坐飞机 (zuò fēi jī). 坐 = to sit/ride; 飞机 = airplane. Compare 坐地铁 (take the subway) and 坐出租车 (take a taxi).

4. 这边 🔊

► Pinyin
zhè biān
► Answer
C. this side / over here — 这边 (zhè biān). Direction words: 这边 (here), 那边 (there), 哪边 (which side), 下边 (below).

5. 上海菜 🔊

► Pinyin
Shàng hǎi cài
► Answer
C. Shanghai cuisine — 上海菜 (Shàng hǎi cài). 菜 = food/dish/cuisine. Compare 四川菜 (Sichuan), 韩国菜 (Korean), 广东话 (Cantonese language — different 菜!).
Section 2: English → Chinese

Read the English. Choose the correct Hanzi. Use 🔊 on each option to hear it. Toggle ► Pinyin to reveal readings.

1. below / underneath

► Pinyin
A. 可靠 — kě kào
B. 哪边 — nǎ biān
C. 下边 — xià biān
D. 层 — céng
► Answer
C. 下边 (xià biān) = below/underneath. 上边 = above, 下边 = below. 层 is a floor counter (e.g. 第三层 = 3rd floor).

2. reliable

► Pinyin
A. 可靠 — kě kào
B. 下边 — xià biān
C. 另一个 — lìng yī ge
D. 哪边 — nǎ biān
► Answer
A. 可靠 (kě kào) = reliable, dependable. 可 means “can be”; 靠 means “to lean on/depend on”. So 可靠 literally = “can be depended on.”

3. which side

► Pinyin
A. 层 — céng
B. 哪边 — nǎ biān
C. 下边 — xià biān
D. 可靠 — kě kào
► Answer
B. 哪边 (nǎ biān) = which side? Question word. 哪 = which (like 哪里 = where). 这边 / 那边 / 哪边 form a complete set.

4. floor / layer

► Pinyin
A. 另一个 — lìng yī ge
B. 哪边 — nǎ biān
C. 可靠 — kě kào
D. 层 — céng
► Answer
D. 层 (céng) = floor/layer/storey. Used as a measure word: 第三层 = 3rd floor. Also in 层次 (hierarchy) and 层层叠叠 (layer upon layer).

5. another one

► Pinyin
A. 层 — céng
B. 可靠 — kě kào
C. 另一个 — lìng yī ge
D. 下边 — xià biān
► Answer
C. 另一个 (lìng yī ge) = another one. 另 = other/another, 一个 = one (item). Useful: 另一个 = another one; 其他的 = other (general).
Section 3: Fill in the Blank

Choose the word that best completes the sentence. Use 🔊 to hear the full sentence with the correct answer filled in.

1. 我可以把文件______给你。 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒ kě yǐ bǎ wénjiàn ______ gěi nǐ。
► Answer
B. 发 — 我可以把文件发给你。
Wǒ kě yǐ bǎ wénjiàn fā gěi nǐ。
I can send the file to you. — 发 here means to send (digitally). 把 + object + verb = a 把-sentence moving the object before the verb.

2. 这个价格很______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Zhè ge jiàgé hěn ______。
► Answer
A. 合理 — 这个价格很合理。
Zhè ge jiàgé hěn hé lǐ。
This price is very reasonable. — 合理 = rational/reasonable. Opposite: 不合理 = unreasonable. 有道理 (yǒu dàolǐ) is another way to say ‘that makes sense.’

3. 地址在我手机里，______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Dìzhǐ zài wǒ shǒujī lǐ，______。
► Answer
B. 我发给你 — 地址在我手机里，我发给你。
Dìzhǐ zài wǒ shǒujī lǐ，wǒ fā gěi nǐ。
The address is on my phone, I’ll send it to you. — 我发给你 is a fixed handy phrase. 发给 = send to. You can substitute: 我发给他 (I’ll send it to him).

4. 从公司到家，我每天______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Cóng gōngsī dào jiā，wǒ měitiān ______。
► Answer
C. 坐地铁 — 从公司到家，我每天坐地铁。
Cóng gōngsī dào jiā，wǒ měitiān zuò dì tiě。
From work to home, I take the subway every day. — 坐 + vehicle = to ride/take that vehicle. 坐地铁 (subway), 坐飞机 (plane), 坐公共汽车 (bus). 从…到… = from…to…

5. 洗手间在______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Xǐshǒujiān zài ______。
► Answer
A. 那边 — 洗手间在那边。
Xǐshǒujiān zài nà biān。
The bathroom is over there. — 洗手间 = bathroom/restroom (literally ‘wash-hand room’). 那边 points to a vague direction; 那儿 is more specific (right there).
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: “I learned five new words in today’s lesson.”

► Answer
今天的课我学了5个新词。 🔊
Jīntiān de kè wǒ xué le wǔ ge xīn cí。
In today’s lesson I learned five new words.
词 (cí) = word/vocabulary item. 新词 = new word. Time expressions (今天的课) come before the subject or at the start in Chinese.

2. Translate: “What do you want to do in the future?”

► Answer
你以后想做什么？ 🔊
Nǐ yǐhòu xiǎng zuò shénme？
What do you want to do in the future?
以后 follows the subject (你以后), unlike English where ‘in the future’ often comes at the end. It can also open a sentence: 以后我想去中国。

3. Translate: “I remember you said you liked Shanghai food.”

► Answer
我记得你说你喜欢上海菜。 🔊
Wǒ jìde nǐ shuō nǐ xǐhuān Shànghǎi cài。
I remember you said you liked Shanghai food.
我记得 introduces the remembered content directly. No 说 is required if you want a shorter version: 我记得你喜欢上海菜。

4. Translate: “He just got off work and is very tired.”

► Answer
他刚下班，很累。 🔊
Tā gāng xiàbān，hěn lèi。
He just got off work and is very tired.
刚 directly precedes the verb (刚下班 = just got off work). Don’t confuse with 刚才 (gāng cái) = just a moment ago (refers to something slightly further back in time).

5. Translate: “Can we order some shumai? The shumai here is really delicious.”

► Answer
我们可以点烧卖吗？这里的烧卖很好吃。 🔊
Wǒmen kěyǐ diǎn shāomài ma？Zhèlǐ de shāomài hěn hǎochī。
Can we order some shumai? The shumai here is really delicious.
烧卖 (shāo mài) = shumai, the open-top dim sum dumpling. Common in Shanghai-style restaurants. 点 here means ‘to order’ (as in ordering from a menu).
