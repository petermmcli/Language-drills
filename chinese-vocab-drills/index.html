Section 1: Chinese → English

1. 橄榄球 🔊

► Pinyin
gǎn lǎn qiú
► Answer
C. rugby / American football — 橄榄球 (gǎn lǎn qiú). 橄榄 (gǎnlǎn) means olive, and 球 (qiú) means ball — the shape of a rugby ball resembles an olive. Don't confuse with 篮球 (lánqiú) = basketball.

2. 自动扶梯 🔊

► Pinyin
zì dòng fú tī
► Answer
D. escalator — 自动扶梯 (zì dòng fú tī). 自动 = automatic, 扶梯 = supported staircase. Note: 梯 alone = ladder/stairs; 扶梯 = escalator or assisted stairs.

3. 苹果怎么卖 🔊

► Pinyin
píng guǒ zěn me mài
► Answer
B. How much are the apples? — 苹果怎么卖 (píng guǒ zěn me mài). Literally 'apples how are they sold?' — a natural way to ask the price per unit in a market. 卖 (mài) = to sell; contrast 买 (mǎi) = to buy.

4. 一个大一点儿的 🔊

► Pinyin
yī gè dà yī diǎr de
► Answer
B. a slightly bigger one — 一个大一点儿的 (yī gè dà yī diǎer de). The pattern Adj+一点儿 = a bit more Adj. Here: a bit bigger. Adding 的 at the end nominalizes it: 'the one that is a bit bigger.'

5. 说得很好 🔊

► Pinyin
shuō de hěn hǎo
► Answer
D. speaks / said it very well — 说得很好 (shuō de hěn hǎo). This is the V+得+Adv pattern (resultative complement): verb + 得 + how-well. Contrast 说得不好 (speaks poorly).
Section 2: English → Chinese

1. should say / one should say

► Pinyin
A. 重复 — chóng fù
B. 这次 — zhè cì
C. 应该说 — yīng gāi shuō
D. 动词 — dòng cí
► Answer
C. 应该说 (yīng gāi shuō) — should say. 应该 (yīnggiāi) is the modal 'should/ought to.' Compare: 重复 = repeat, 这次 = this time, 动词 = verb.

2. this time

► Pinyin
A. 这次 — zhè cì
B. 活动 — huó dòng
C. 运动会 — yùn dòng huì
D. 视频 — shì pín
► Answer
A. 这次 (zhè cì) — this time. 次 (cì) is a measure word for occurrences/times. Compare: 上次 (last time), 下次 (next time).

3. Are you thinking of buying water? / Would you like some water?

► Pinyin
A. 苹果怎么卖 — píng guǒ zěn me mài
B. 是不是想买水 — shì bú shì xiǎng mǎi shuǐ
C. 一共多少钱 — yī gòng duō shǎo qián
D. 那儿没了吗 — nàr méi le ma
► Answer
B. 是不是想买水 (shì bú shì xiǎng mǎi shuǐ) — Are you thinking of buying water? 是不是 is an A-not-A question pattern. 买 (mǎi) = buy; 卖 (mài) = sell — easy to mix up!

4. video

► Pinyin
A. 运动会 — yùn dòng huì
B. 视频 — shì pín
C. 活动 — huó dòng
D. 那儿没了吗 — nàr méi le ma
► Answer
B. 视频 (shì pín) — video. 视 = vision/sight; 频 = frequency/signal. Compare: 活动 (event/activity), 运动会 (sports day).

5. Here's your 25 yuan change

► Pinyin
A. 一共多少钱 — yī gòng duō shǎo qián
B. 苹果怎么卖 — píng guǒ zěn me mài
C. 找您25块 — zhǎo nín 25 kuài
D. 一个大一点儿的 — yī gè dà yī diǎr de
► Answer
C. 找您25块 (zhǎo nín 25 kuài) — Here's your 25 yuan change. 找 here means 'to give change' (not 'to look for'). 您 is the polite 'you'. Note: 一共多少钱 is the customer's question; 找您X块 is the cashier's reply.
Section 3: Fill in the Blank

Choose the word that best completes the sentence. Use 🔊 to hear the full sentence with the correct answer filled in.

1. 她平时______，但有时候也去运动。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā píng shí ______，dàn yǒu shí hòu yě qù yùn dòng。
► Answer
B. 不经常跳舞 — 她平时不经常跳舞，但有时候也去运动。
Tā píng shí bù jīng cháng tiào wǔ，dàn yǒu shí hòu yě qù yùn dòng。
She doesn't often dance, but sometimes goes to exercise. — 不经常 (bù jīngcháng) = not often/frequently. 平时 = normally/usually. 有时候 = sometimes.

2. 他中文______，需要多练习。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā zhōng wén ______，xū yào duō liàn xí。
► Answer
A. 说得不好 — 他中文说得不好，需要多练习。
Tā zhōng wén shuō de bù hǎo，xū yào duō liàn xí。
His Chinese is not very good — he needs more practice. — V+得+Adj complement: 说得不好 = speaks poorly. 中文 is the topic. 需要多练习 = needs to practice more.

3. 我弟弟______，所以需要多运动。 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒ dì di ______，suǒ yǐ xū yào duō yùn dòng。
► Answer
B. 吃得很多 — 我弟弟吃得很多，所以需要多运动。
Wǒ dì di chī de hěn duō，suǒ yǐ xū yào duō yùn dòng。
My younger brother eats a lot, so he needs to exercise more. — 吃得很多 uses V+得+Adv again. 所以 (suǒyǐ) = so/therefore. 多运动 = exercise more.

4. "吃"和"喝"在中文里都是______。 🔊

► Pinyin
"chī" hé "hē" zài zhōng wén lǐ dōu shì ______。
► Answer
C. 动词 — "吃"和"喝"在中文里都是动词。
"chī" hé "hē" zài zhōng wén lǐ dōu shì dòng cí。
"Eat" and "drink" are both verbs in Chinese. — 动词 (dòngcí) = verb (literally 'action word'). 都是 = are both. In Chinese grammar class, you'll also see 名词 (noun) and 形容词 (adjective).

5. 请问这些苹果______？ 🔊

► Pinyin
Qǐng wèn zhè xiē píng guǒ ______？
► Answer
B. 一共多少钱 — 请问这些苹果一共多少钱？
Qǐng wèn zhè xiē píng guǒ yī gòng duō shǎo qián？
Excuse me, how much are all these apples in total? — 一共多少钱 asks for the total price. 苹果怎么卖 asks the price per unit. 请问 = Excuse me (polite intro). 这些 = these (plural).
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: "Is there none left over there?"

► Answer
那儿没了吗？ 🔊
Nàr méi le ma？
Is there none left over there? / Is it all gone?
那儿 = over there (erhua). 没了 = there's none left / it ran out. 吗 turns it into a yes/no question. This phrase is handy in a shop when something seems to be out of stock.

2. Translate: "Our school has a sports day next week."

► Answer
我们学校下周有运动会。 🔊
Wǒ men xué xiào xià zhōu yǒu yùn dòng huì。
Our school has a sports day next week.
运动会 (yùndònghuì) = sports day / athletic meet. 下周 = next week. 有运动会 = there is a sports meet. Time expressions come before the verb.

3. Translate: "She doesn't often drink coffee."

► Answer
她不经常喝咖啡。 🔊
Tā bù jīng cháng hē kā fēi。
She doesn't often drink coffee.
不经常 = not often. It goes directly before the verb phrase. Compare: 经常 alone = often/frequently. 喝咖啡 = drink coffee.

4. Translate: "Please repeat that."

► Answer
请重复一下。 🔊
Qǐng chóng fù yī xià。
Please repeat that.
重复 (chóngfù) = to repeat. 一下 softens commands and makes them more casual/polite — literally 'one time.' 请 = please. Together: 请重复一下 is the natural classroom phrase.

5. Translate: "What activities are there this weekend?"

► Answer
这个周末有什么活动？ 🔊
Zhè gè zhōu mò yǒu shén me huó dòng？
What activities are there this weekend?
有什么X? = what X is there / is there any X? 这个周末 = this weekend (周末 = weekend, 周 = week, 末 = end). 活动 can mean activities, events, or even 'to exercise.'
