Section 3: Fill in the Blank

Choose the word that best completes the sentence. Use 🔊 to hear the full sentence with the correct answer filled in.

1. 我可以用______付钱吗？ 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒ kěyǐ yòng ______ fùqián ma？
► Answer
A. 卡 — 我可以用卡付钱吗？
Wǒ kěyǐ yòng kǎ fùqián ma？
Can I pay by card? — 用 = use/with. 付钱 = pay money. 卡 covers credit cards, debit cards, transit cards, etc.

2. 这件衣服很______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Zhè jiàn yīfu hěn ______。
► Answer
B. 好看 — 这件衣服很好看。
Zhè jiàn yīfu hěn hǎokàn。
This piece of clothing is very pretty. — 好看 literally means 'good to look at'. Use for things that are visually attractive — clothes, places, people.

3. 今天是我的______，我很开心！ 🔊

► Pinyin
Jīntiān shì wǒ de ______，wǒ hěn kāixīn！
► Answer
C. 生日 — 今天是我的生日，我很开心！
Jīntiān shì wǒ de shēngrì，wǒ hěn kāixīn！
Today is my birthday, I'm very happy! — 生日快乐 = Happy Birthday! 生 = born, 日 = day/sun.

4. 不要担心，只是______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Bú yào dānxīn，zhǐshì ______。
► Answer
A. 小病 — 不要担心，只是小病。
Bú yào dānxīn，zhǐshì xiǎo bìng。
Don't worry, it's just a minor illness. — 小 (small/little) + 病 (illness) = minor illness. 感冒 = cold/flu is more specific, while 小病 is a general reassurance.

5. 我______去看电影，你呢？ 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒ ______ qù kàn diànyǐng，nǐ ne？
► Answer
B. 想 — 我想去看电影，你呢？
Wǒ xiǎng qù kàn diànyǐng，nǐ ne？
I want to go see a movie. What about you? — 想 before a verb means 'want to'. 能 = can (ability), 怕 = afraid. 你呢 = what about you? (topic continuation).
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: "My computer is very slow."

► Answer
我的电脑很慢。 🔊
Wǒ de diànnǎo hěn màn。
My computer is very slow.
电脑 literally means 'electric brain'. 的 makes 我 possessive. 很 + adjective is the standard 'is very…' structure.

2. Translate: "Today's weather is really good!"

► Answer
今天的天气真好！ 🔊
Jīntiān de tiānqì zhēn hǎo！
Today's weather is really good!
天气 = weather (lit. 'sky air'). 真 = really/truly (stronger than 很). 今天的 uses 的 to show possession/description.

3. Translate: "Is the doctor male or female?"

► Answer
医生是男的还是女的？ 🔊
Yīshēng shì nán de háishi nǚ de？
Is the doctor male or female?
男的 / 女的 (male / female) uses 的 to nominalize the adjective. 还是 between two options means 'or' in a question.

4. Translate: "At the intersection, turn left."

► Answer
在路口左转。 🔊
Zài lùkǒu zuǒzhuǎn。
At the intersection, turn left.
路口 = intersection (路 = road + 口 = mouth/opening). 左转 = turn left, 右转 = turn right. 在 marks location.

5. Translate: "I don't know which road to take."

► Answer
我不知道走哪条路。 🔊
Wǒ bù zhīdào zǒu nǎ tiáo lù。
I don't know which road to take.
条 is the measure word for roads/rivers/long things. 哪 = which. 走 here means 'go by / take (a road)'.
