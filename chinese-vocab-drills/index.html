1. receive an email

► Pinyin

► Answer

2. receive the exam email

► Pinyin
A. 收到邮件 — shōu dào yóujiàn
B. 收到考试的邮件 — shōu dào kǎoshì de yóujiàn
C. 收到考试的通知 — shōu dào kǎoshì de tōngzhī
D. 开卷考试 — kāi juàn kǎoshì
► Answer
B. 收到考试的邮件 — 收到 (receive) + 考试的 (of the exam) + 邮件 (email). Compare: 收到考试的通知 = receive the exam notification.

3. everyone

► Pinyin
A. 大家 — dà jiā
B. 它 — tā
C. 报名 — bào míng
D. 宾馆 — bīn guǎn
► Answer
A. 大家 (dà jiā) — everyone/everybody. Literally "big home/family," figuratively "all of us." Very common in speech.

4. it (referring to a thing or animal)

► Pinyin

B. 它 — tā
C. 大家 — dà jiā
D. 说话 — shuōhuà
► Answer
B. 它 (tā) — "it," used for animals and objects. Note: 他 (he), 她 (she), 它 (it) are all pronounced tā — different characters, same sound.

B. 开卷考试 (kāi juàn kǎoshì) — open book exam. 开卷 = open book (lit. open the volume); 考试 = exam.
Section 3: Fill in the Blank

Choose the word that best completes the sentence. Use 🔊 to hear the full sentence with the correct answer filled in.

1. 他觉得______，所以他总是找最难的工作。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā juéde ______，suǒyǐ tā zǒngshì zhǎo zuì nán de gōngzuò。
► Answer
C. 压力越大越好 — 他觉得压力越大越好，所以他总是找最难的工作。
Tā juéde yālì yuè dà yuè hǎo，suǒyǐ tā zǒngshì zhǎo zuì nán de gōngzuò。
He thinks the more pressure the better, so he always looks for the hardest jobs. — 越…越… = the more…the more…. 压力越大越好 = the more pressure, the better. 所以 = therefore/so.

2. 你快点______，我们等着你。 🔊

► Pinyin
Nǐ kuài diǎn ______，wǒmen děngzhe nǐ。
► Answer
B. 回来 — 你快点回来，我们等着你。
Nǐ kuài diǎn huílái，wǒmen děngzhe nǐ。
Hurry up and come back — we're waiting for you. — 回来 = come back (toward speaker). 回去 = go back (away from speaker). 等着 = waiting for.

3. 他明天想______老家看看。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā míngtiān xiǎng ______ lǎojiā kànkan。
► Answer
C. 回去 — 他明天想回去老家看看。
Tā míngtiān xiǎng huíqù lǎojiā kànkan。
He wants to go back to his hometown tomorrow for a look. — 回去 = go back (away from speaker). 老家 = hometown. 看看 = have a look (reduplicated = brief action).

4. 我每天都喜欢______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒ měitiān dōu xǐhuān ______。
► Answer
A. 画画 — 我每天都喜欢画画。
Wǒ měitiān dōu xǐhuān huàhuà。
I like to draw/paint every day. — 画画 = to draw or paint (verb + object using same character). 说话 is a similar pattern: 说 (speak) + 话 (speech/words).

5. 他______我先吃饭。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā ______ wǒ xiān chīfàn。
► Answer
C. 让 — 他让我先吃饭。
Tā ràng wǒ xiān chīfàn。
He let me eat first. — 让 + person + verb = let/allow someone to do something. Structure: Subject 让 Object Verb.
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: "My friend is currently eating at a restaurant."

► Answer
我朋友正在餐厅吃饭。 🔊
Wǒ péngyǒu zhèngzài cāntīng chīfàn。
My friend is currently eating at a restaurant.
正在 + verb = action in progress right now (like English -ing). 正在 comes before the verb. Often paired with 呢 at the end: 正在吃饭呢。

2. Translate: "Did you receive the notification about the exam?"

► Answer
你收到考试的通知了吗？ 🔊
Nǐ shōudào kǎoshì de tōngzhī le ma？
Did you receive the notification about the exam?
收到 = receive (resultative: 收 collect + 到 reach). 了 after the verb indicates completion. 通知 = notification/notice.

3. Translate: "I'm busy tomorrow, I can't go."

► Answer
我明天很忙，不能去。 🔊
Wǒ míngtiān hěn máng，bù néng qù。
I'm busy tomorrow, I can't go.
Time words come right after the subject. 明天 (tomorrow) → 后天 (day after tomorrow) → 大后天 (2 days after tomorrow).

4. Translate: "Please don't talk during the exam."

► Answer
考试的时候请不要说话。 🔊
Kǎoshì de shíhou qǐng bú yào shuōhuà。
Please don't talk during the exam.
说话 = speak/talk. …的时候 = when/during. 不要 = don't (prohibition). 请 softens the request.

5. Translate: "I can't find my pencil."

► Answer
我找不到我的铅笔。 🔊
Wǒ zhǎo bu dào wǒ de qiānbǐ。
I can't find my pencil.
找不到 = can't find (potential complement: verb + 不 + resultative = unable to complete). 铅笔 = pencil; 钢笔 = fountain pen; 圆珠笔 = ballpoint pen.
