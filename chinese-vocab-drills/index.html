1. 先坐地铁，再换公交车 🔊

► Pinyin
Xiān zuò dìtiě，zài huàn gōngjiāochē
► Answer
B. First take the subway, then transfer to the bus — 先坐地铁，再换公交车 (xiān zuò dìtiě，zài huàn gōngjiāochē) is a common direction-giving phrase. 先…再… means "first…then…" 换 = to transfer/change.

2. 过 🔊

► Pinyin
guò
► Answer
B. to cross / to pass / (completion particle) — 过 (guò) has several meanings: 过马路 = cross the road; 去过 = have been to (experience aspect); also used in 过去 (guòqù = the past). Don't confuse with 国 (guó = country).

3. 公交车 🔊

► Pinyin
gōngjiāochē
► Answer
B. public bus — 公交车 (gōngjiāochē) = public transportation vehicle. Compare: 地铁 (dìtiě) = subway, 出租车 (chūzūchē) = taxi, 自行车 (zìxíngchē) = bicycle.
Section 2: English → Chinese

Read the English. Choose the correct Hanzi. Use 🔊 on each option to hear it. Toggle ► Pinyin to reveal readings.

1. say it again / repeat

► Pinyin
A. 遍 — biàn
B. 就到了 — jiù dào le
C. 再说一遍 — zài shuō yī biàn
D. 不错 — bùcuò
► Answer
C. 再说一遍 (zài shuō yī biàn) — literally "again say one time." 再 = again, 遍 = time/round. Use this when asking someone to repeat something fully. Compare: 再说一次 is also acceptable.

2. time / round (measure word for complete actions)

► Pinyin
A. 最后 — zuìhòu
B. 遍 — biàn
C. 再说一遍 — zài shuō yī biàn
D. 就到了 — jiù dào le
► Answer
B. 遍 (biàn) — measure word for complete actions, from start to finish. 看了一遍 = watched it through once. Distinct from 次 (cì = occurrence/time, less emphasis on completion) and 下 (xià = quick attempt).

3. almost there / will arrive soon

► Pinyin
A. 就到了 — jiù dào le
B. 中学 — zhōngxué
C. 遍 — biàn
D. 最后 — zuìhòu
► Answer
A. 就到了 (jiù dào le) — "just about to arrive / almost there." 就 here adds immediacy (very soon). Common response when someone asks 你到哪儿了？(Where are you?)
Section 3: Fill in the Blank

Choose the word that best completes the sentence. Use 🔊 to hear the full sentence with the correct answer filled in.

1. 我家附近有一所______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒ jiā fùjìn yǒu yī suǒ ______。
► Answer
B. 中学 — 我家附近有一所中学。
Wǒ jiā fùjìn yǒu yī suǒ zhōngxué。
There is a middle school near my home. — 所 (suǒ) is the measure word for institutions: 一所学校, 一所医院. 中学 = middle school (grades 7-12 in China). 附近 = nearby.

2. ______，他终于明白了。 🔊

► Pinyin
______，tā zhōngyú míngbai le。
► Answer
C. 最后 — 最后，他终于明白了。
Zuìhòu，tā zhōngyú míngbai le。
Finally, he came to understand. — 最后 (zuìhòu) = lastly / in the end, used as a time expression at the start or end of a sentence. 终于 (zhōngyú) = at long last (often follows 最后 for emphasis).
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: "I ride a bicycle to work every day."

► Answer
我每天骑自行车去上班。 🔊
Wǒ měitiān qí zìxíngchē qù shàngbān。
I ride a bicycle to work every day.
骑 (qí) is the verb for riding two-wheeled vehicles: 骑自行车 (bicycle), 骑摩托车 (motorcycle). For cars and buses, use 坐 or 开. 上班 = to go to work.

2. Translate: "Your Mandarin is really not bad — keep it up!"

► Answer
你的普通话真不错，继续加油！ 🔊
Nǐ de pǔtōnghuà zhēn bùcuò，jìxù jiāyóu！
Your Mandarin is really not bad — keep it up!
不错 (bùcuò) literally means "not wrong" but functions as "pretty good / not bad." 继续 (jìxù) = to continue, 加油 (jiāyóu) = to cheer on / keep going.
