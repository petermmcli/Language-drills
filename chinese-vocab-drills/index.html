Section 1: Chinese → English

Look at the Hanzi. Choose the correct English meaning. Use the Pinyin toggle if you need help.

1. 兼职 🔊

► Pinyin
jiān zhí
► Answer
C. part-time job — 兼职 (jiān zhí) means to work part-time or a side job. 兼 = hold concurrently, 职 = position/job.

2. 一起去 🔊

► Pinyin
yī qǐ qù
► Answer
A. go together — 一起去 (yī qǐ qù) = go together. 一起 = together, 去 = go.

3. 把 🔊

► Pinyin
bǎ
► Answer
B. grammar particle (disposal) — 把 (bǎ) is the disposal marker. Example: 我把作业做完了 = I finished my homework. It can also mean a handle or to hold.

4. 旧 🔊

► Pinyin
jiù
► Answer
C. old (things) — 旧 (jiù) = old/used for objects. Don’t confuse with 老 (lǎo) which refers to old people. 新 is the opposite.

5. 驾考 🔊

► Pinyin
jià kǎo
► Answer
B. driving test — 驾考 (jià kǎo) = driving exam. 驾 = to drive, 考 = exam. 考驾考 = take the test, 通过驾考 = pass the test.
Section 2: English → Chinese

Read the English. Choose the correct Hanzi. Use 🔊 on each option to hear it. Toggle ► Pinyin to reveal readings.

1. pass the driving test

► Pinyin
A. 兼职 — jiān zhí
B. 通过驾考 — tōng guò jià kǎo
C. 考驾考 — kǎo jià kǎo
D. 驾照 — jià zhào
► Answer
B. 通过驾考 — 通过 = pass/get through, 驾考 = driving test. 考驾考 = take the test; 驾照 = driving license.

2. here is your change of ... yuan

► Pinyin
A. 找您……块 — zhǎo nín……kuài
B. 请问怎么付錢？ — qǐng wèn zěn me fù qián？
C. 一共多少錢 — yī gòng duō shao qián
D. 没有零錢 — méi yǒu líng qián
► Answer
A. 找您…块 — 找 here = give change (not look for). 您 = polite you. Cashier says: 找您2块 = here’s 2 yuan change.

3. send / give as a gift

► Pinyin
A. 买 — mǎi
B. 带 — dài
C. 交 — jiāo
D. 送 — sòng
► Answer
D. 送 (sòng) — means to give a gift AND to deliver/escort. 送礼物 = give a gift. 带 = bring/carry. 交 = hand in/submit. 买 = buy.

4. fresh

► Pinyin
A. 新 — xīn
B. 新鲜 — xīn xiān
C. 好吃 — hǎo chī
D. 干净 — gān jìng
► Answer
B. 新鲜 (xīn xiān) — 新鲜 specifically means fresh (food, air, etc.). 新 alone = new. 新鲜空气 = fresh air, 新鲜水果 = fresh fruit.

5. what do you like to eat

► Pinyin
A. 喜欢吃什么 — xǐ huān chī shén me
B. 你想吃什么 — nǐ xiǎng chī shén me
C. 要点什么 — yào diǎn shén me
D. 吃什么好 — chī shén me hǎo
► Answer
A. 喜欢吃什么 — 喜欢 = like/prefer. 你想吃什么 = what do you want to eat (right now). 喜欢 implies habitual preference; 想 implies current desire.
Section 3: Fill in the Blank

Choose the word that best completes the sentence. Use 🔊 to hear the full sentence with the correct answer filled in.

1. 我们周末去______，好吗？ 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒ men zhōu mò qù ______，hǎo ma？
► Answer
A. KTV — 我们周末去KTV，好吗？
Wǒ men zhōu mò qù KTV，hǎo ma？
Let's go to karaoke this weekend, OK? — KTV (karaoke TV) is the Chinese term for karaoke bars. 好吗 at the end is a soft suggestion: how about it?

2. 这个苹果______很甜。 🔊

► Pinyin
Zhè ge píng guǒ ______ hěn tián。
► Answer
B. 吃起来 — 这个苹果吃起来很甜。
Zhè ge píng guǒ chī qǐ lái hěn tián。
This apple tastes very sweet. — V+起来 describes how something seems when you do the action: 吃起来 = tastes like, 看起来 = looks like, 摸起来 = feels like (to touch).

3. 他长得很______他爸爸。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā zhǎng de hěn ______ tā bà ba。
► Answer
A. 像 — 他长得很像他爸爸。
Tā zhǎng de hěn xiàng tā bà ba。
He looks very much like his dad. — 像 = to resemble. 长得像 means to have grown to look like someone. 是 (to be) and 跟 (with/and) don’t work here.

4. 她的头发是______色的。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā de tóu fa shì ______ sè de。
► Answer
B. 黑 — 她的头发是黑色的。
Tā de tóu fa shì hēi sè de。
Her hair is black. — Colors: 黑 black, 白 white, 红 red, 黄 yellow, 蓝 blue. Add 色 to make a color noun: 黑色 = the color black.

5. 路上有冰，请______！ 🔊

► Pinyin
Lù shàng yǒu bīng，qǐng ______！
► Answer
B. 小心 — 路上有冰，请小心！
Lù shàng yǒu bīng，qǐng xiǎo xīn！
There's ice on the road, please be careful! — 小心 = be careful. 开心 = happy. 放心 = don't worry. All end in 心 (heart) but mean very different things!
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: "Let's buy one more."

► Answer
我们再买一个吧。 🔊
Wǒ men zài mǎi yī gè ba。
Let's buy one more.
再 here = again / one more time. 吧 softens the suggestion. 再买一个 = buy one more item.

2. Translate: "She has gotten thinner recently."

► Answer
她最近瘦了。 🔊
Tā zuì jìn shòu le。
She has gotten thinner recently.
瘦 (小心) = thin/slim. 了 at the end marks a change of state. The opposite is 胖 (pàng) = fat/chubby.

3. Translate: "How much is this apple?" (at a market stall)

► Answer
这个苹果怎么卖？ 🔊
Zhè ge píng guǒ zěn me mài？
How much is this apple? / How are these apples sold?
怎么卖 = how is it sold? (asks about price per unit, e.g. per jin). Natural at a market. 卖 = to sell (卖 vs 买 buy: 卖 has an extra stroke on top).

4. Translate: "I want to buy some fruit."

► Answer
我想买点儿水果。 🔊
Wǒ xiǎng mǎi diǎnr shuǐ guǒ。
I want to buy some fruit.
点儿 (diǎnr) = a little / some. 买点儿水果 = buy some fruit. The 儿 makes it colloquial and Beijing-flavored.

5. Translate: "Let's do a video call!"

► Answer
我们打视频电话吧！ 🔊
Wǒ men dǎ shì pín diàn huà ba！
Let's do a video call!
视频电话 = video call (lit. video telephone). Use 打 as the verb: 打电话 = make a phone call, 打视频电话 = make a video call.
