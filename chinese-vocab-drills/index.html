Section 1: Chinese → English

1. 得 🔊

► Pinyin
de
► Answer
B. verb complement marker — 得 (de) connects a verb to its result or degree: 说得很好 = speaks very well; 跑得很快 = runs very fast. Three pronunciations: de (complement), děi (must), dé (obtain).

2. 暑假 🔊

► Pinyin
shǔ jià
► Answer
C. summer vacation — 暑假 (shǔ jià). 暑 = summer heat, 假 = holiday. Contrast: 寒假 = winter vacation.

3. 店员 🔊

► Pinyin
diàn yuán
► Answer
A. shop assistant / store clerk — 店员 (diàn yuán). 店 = shop, 员 = staff member. Contrast: 服务员 = waiter/service staff.

4. 一共多少钱 🔊

► Pinyin
Yī gòng duōshao qián
► Answer
C. How much is it altogether? — 一共多少钱: 一共 = altogether/in total, 多少钱 = how much money. Essential shopping phrase.

5. 不经常喝咖啡 🔊

► Pinyin
Bù jīngcháng hē kāfēi
► Answer
B. (I) don't often drink coffee — 不经常 = not often/seldom. Pattern: 不经常 + verb. Contrast: 经常 + verb = often/frequently.

► Answer

2. are you thinking of buying water?

► Pinyin
A. 我和你一起去 — Wǒ hé nǐ yī qǐ qù

► Answer
C. 是不是想买水 — 是不是 = are you / is it the case that (polite question tag). 想买水 = thinking of buying water. A natural shop assistant phrase.

3. never / not ever

B. 应该 — yīng gāi

Section 3: Fill in the Blank

1. 这家水果店的______很甜。 🔊

► Pinyin
Zhè jiā shuǐguǒ diàn de ______ hěn tián。
► Answer
B. 苹果 — 这家水果店的苹果很甜。
Zhè jiā shuǐguǒ diàn de píngguǒ hěn tián。
The apples at this fruit store are very sweet. — 苹果 (píng guǒ, t2-t3) = apple. 甜 = sweet. 这家 uses classifier 家 for shops/establishments.

2. A: 周末去不去逛商店？B: ______！ 🔊

► Pinyin
A: Zhōumò qù bù qù guàng shāngdiàn？B: ______！
► Answer
C. 我和你一起去 — A: 周末去不去逛商店？B: 我和你一起去！
A: Zhōumò qù bù qù guàng shāngdiàn？B: Wǒ hé nǐ yī qǐ qù！
A: Are you going shopping this weekend? B: I'll go with you! — 和……一起 = together with. 我和你一起去 = I'll go with you. 逛商店 = browse/go shopping.

3. 学中文______每天练习。 🔊

► Pinyin
Xué Zhōngwén ______ měi tiān liànxí。
► Answer
C. 应该 — 学中文应该每天练习。
Xué Zhōngwén yīnggāi měi tiān liànxí。
When studying Chinese, you should practise every day. — 应该 (yīng gāi, t1-t1) = should/ought to. 练习 = practise. Compare: 要 = need to/want to, 得 (děi) = must.

4. 对不起，我没听清楚，你能______吗？ 🔊

► Pinyin
Duìbuqǐ，wǒ méi tīng qīngchu，nǐ néng ______ ma？
► Answer
A. 重复一下 — 对不起，我没听清楚，你能重复一下吗？
Duìbuqǐ，wǒ méi tīng qīngchu，nǐ néng chóngfù yīxià ma？
Sorry, I didn't hear clearly. Could you repeat that? — 重复一下 (chóng fù yī xià) = repeat once. 一下 softens the request. 没听清楚 = didn't hear clearly. Classroom / conversation essential.

5. 我想要一______可乐。 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒ xiǎng yào yī ______ kělè。
► Answer
B. 瓶 — 我想要一瓶可乐。
Wǒ xiǎng yào yī píng kělè。
I'd like a bottle of cola. — 瓶 (píng, t2) = measure word for bottles. 杯 = cup/glass, 斤 = jin (weight unit). 一瓶可乐 is a set phrase in your vocabulary.
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: "These apples are 3 yuan per jin."

► Answer
这些苹果三块钱一斤。 🔊
Zhèxiē píngguǒ sān kuài qián yī jīn。
These apples are 3 yuan per jin.
斤 (jīn, t1) = Chinese unit of weight, 500g. Market price formula: X块钱一斤 = X yuan per jin. Also: 苹果怎么卖 = how much are the apples?

2. Translate: "I want to go to the fruit store."

► Answer
我想去水果店。 🔊
Wǒ xiǎng qù shuǐguǒ diàn。
I want to go to the fruit store.
想去 = want to go. 水果店 = fruit store. Also in vocab: 便利店 = convenience store, 衣服店 = clothing store.

3. Translate: "How much are the watermelons?"

► Answer
西瓜怎么卖？ 🔊
Xīguā zěnme mài？
How much are the watermelons?
怎么卖 = how much is it? (lit. how do you sell it?) Natural market phrase. You have 苹果怎么卖 in your vocab — same pattern for any product.

4. Translate: "I'll have a bottle of cola."

► Answer
我要一瓶可乐。 🔊
Wǒ yào yī píng kělè。
I'll have a bottle of cola.
一瓶可乐 is a set phrase in your vocab. 瓶 is the measure word for bottles. 我要… = I'll have / I want.

5. Translate: "Your change is 25 yuan."

► Answer
找您25块。 🔊
Zhǎo nín èrshíwǔ kuài。
Your change is 25 yuan.
找 here = 'give change' (not 'look for'). 您 = polite 'you'. 找您X块 is the standard cashier phrase. Also in vocab: 一共多少钱 = how much altogether.
