1. 斤 🔊

► Pinyin
jīn
► Answer
B. catty (500g unit) — 斤 (jīn) is a traditional Chinese unit of weight equal to 500 grams. Used at markets: 苹果怎么卖？两块五一斤。Don't confuse with 公斤 (gōng jīn) = kilogram (2 斤).

1. type / kind / sort

► Pinyin
A. 个 — ge
B. 元 — yuán
C. 种 — zhǒng
D. 斤 — jīn
► Answer
C. 种 (zhǒng) — type, kind, or sort. It also works as a measure word: 这种咖啡很好喝 (this type of coffee is delicious). 个 is the general measure word for items; 元 is currency; 斤 is weight.

1. 一共五______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Yīgòng wǔ ______。
► Answer
B. 元 — 一共五元。
Yīgòng wǔ yuán。
Five yuan in total. — 元 (yuán) is the formal written form of the currency unit. In speech, 块 (kuài) is more common, but 元 is used on receipts, menus, and in formal contexts. 一共 means 'in total'.
