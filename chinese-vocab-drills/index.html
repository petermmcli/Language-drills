1. 跑步 🔊

► Pinyin
pǎo bù
► Answer
C. to jog / run — 跑步 (pǎo bù) is a verb phrase meaning to jog or go running. 跑 = run, 步 = step/pace. Contrast: 走路 = to walk.

2. 大小 🔊

► Pinyin
dà xiǎo
► Answer
A. size — 大小 (dà xiǎo) lit. big-small, refers to the size of clothing/shoes. Often used in 衣服的大小怎么样？= How does the size fit?

3. 颜色 🔊

► Pinyin
yán sè
► Answer
C. color — 颜色 (yán sè) means color. Common phrase: 有别的颜色吗？= Do you have other colors? Related: 黄色 = yellow, 红色 = red.

4. 鞋 🔊

► Pinyin
xié
► Answer
A. shoes — 鞋 (xié) means shoes. Note the 2nd tone. 一双鞋 = a pair of shoes.

5. 烧卖 🔊

► Pinyin
shāo mài
► Answer
B. siu mai dumpling — 烧卖 (shāo mài) is a steamed open-topped dim sum dumpling filled with pork and shrimp. A classic dim sum item. 点心 = dim sum.

1. shop / store

► Pinyin
A. 颜色 — yán sè
B. 鞋 — xié
C. 店 — diàn
D. 大小 — dà xiǎo
► Answer
C. 店 (diàn) — a shop or store. Often used as a suffix: 衣服店 = clothing store, 书店 = bookstore.

2. to send; to grow (hair/nails)

► Pinyin
A. 刚 — gāng
B. 发 — fā
C. 看情况 — kàn qíng kuàng
D. 试衣间 — shì yī jiān
► Answer
B. 发 (fā) — to send (a message, file) or to grow/emit. 发信息 = send a message; 你可以在Chat里发给我 = you can send it to me in Chat.

3. it depends / see how it goes

► Pinyin
A. 看情况 — kàn qíng kuàng
B. 刚 — gāng
C. 以后 — yǐ hòu
D. 老样子 — lǎo yàng zi
► Answer
A. 看情况 (kàn qíng kuàng) — lit. look at the situation, meaning it depends / we'll see. Don't confuse with 老样子 = same as always.

4. fitting room / changing room

► Pinyin
A. 衣服店 — yī fu diàn
B. 颜色 — yán sè
C. 鞋 — xié
D. 试衣间 — shì yī jiān
► Answer
D. 试衣间 (shì yī jiān) — fitting room or changing room. 试 = try on, 衣 = clothes, 间 = room. Contrast: 衣服店 = clothing store (the whole shop).

5. just (a moment ago) / just now

► Pinyin
A. 以后 — yǐ hòu
B. 刚 — gāng
C. 发 — fā
D. 第 — dì
► Answer
B. 刚 (gāng) — just now, just recently. 我刚到 = I just arrived. 刚才 = just a moment ago. Don't confuse with 以后 = later/in the future.
Section 3: Fill in the Blank

Choose the word that best completes the sentence. Use 🔊 to hear the full sentence with the correct answer filled in.

1. 最近怎么样？—— ______，你呢？ 🔊

► Pinyin
Zuì jìn zěnmeyàng？—— ______，nǐ ne？
► Answer
B. 老样子 — 最近怎么样？—— 老样子，你呢？
Zuì jìn zěnmeyàng？—— lǎo yàng zi，nǐ ne？
How have you been lately? — Same as always, you? — 老样子 is a casual, natural response to 最近怎么样？meaning 'same as always / same old.' Very common in spoken Chinese.

2. 这是我______一次来上海。 🔊

► Pinyin
Zhè shì wǒ ______ yī cì lái Shànghǎi。
► Answer
C. 第 — 这是我第一次来上海。
Zhè shì wǒ dì yī cì lái Shànghǎi。
This is my first time coming to Shanghai. — 第 is the ordinal prefix: 第一 = 1st, 第二 = 2nd, 第三 = 3rd. 第一次 = the first time. 次 = time/instance (counter for actions).

3. A: 我想买这件衣服。B: ______ 🔊

► Pinyin
A: Wǒ xiǎng mǎi zhè jiàn yīfu。B: ______
► Answer
B. 衣服的大小怎么样？ — A: 我想买这件衣服。B: 衣服的大小怎么样？
A: Wǒ xiǎng mǎi zhè jiàn yīfu。B: Yīfu de dàxiǎo zěnmeyàng？
A: I want to buy this piece of clothing. B: How does the size fit? — 衣服的大小怎么样？is a complete shopping phrase — 'How's the size?' Use it in fitting rooms and clothing stores to ask about fit.

4. 学中文的时候，每节课都有很多______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Xué Zhōngwén de shíhòu，měi jié kè dōu yǒu hěn duō ______。
► Answer
B. 新词 — 学中文的时候，每节课都有很多新词。
Xué Zhōngwén de shíhòu，měi jié kè dōu yǒu hěn duō xīn cí。
When studying Chinese, every class has lots of new words. — 新词 = new word/vocabulary. 新 = new, 词 = word. 每节课 = every class/lesson (节 = class period, a measure word for lessons).

5. 她想买新衣服，所以去了______。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā xiǎng mǎi xīn yīfu，suǒyǐ qù le ______。
► Answer
C. 衣服店 — 她想买新衣服，所以去了衣服店。
Tā xiǎng mǎi xīn yīfu，suǒyǐ qù le yīfu diàn。
She wanted to buy new clothes, so she went to a clothing store. — 衣服店 = clothing store. 书店 = bookstore. 试衣间 = fitting room (inside the store). 因为…所以… structure: because…therefore…
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: "Do you have this in a new color?"

► Answer
有没有新的颜色？ 🔊
Yǒu méi yǒu xīn de yán sè？
Do you have this in a new color? / Are there any new colors?
有没有 is the standard 'do you have' pattern. Adding 新 (new) before 颜色 (color) gives a natural shopping question.

2. Translate: "Do you remember the new vocabulary?"

► Answer
你记得新词吗？ 🔊
Nǐ jìde xīn cí ma？
Do you remember the new words?
记得 = to remember (factual). 记住 = to memorise/commit to memory. 忘 = to forget — the opposite of 记得.

3. Translate: "You can send it to me in Chat."

► Answer
你可以在Chat里发给我。 🔊
Nǐ kěyǐ zài Chat lǐ fā gěi wǒ。
You can send it to me in Chat.
Structure: 你可以 (you can) + 在Chat里 (in Chat) + 发给我 (send to me). 在…里 = inside/within. 发给 = send to.

4. Translate: "I'll go to the clothing store later."

► Answer
我以后去衣服店。 🔊
Wǒ yǐhòu qù yīfu diàn。
I'll go to the clothing store later / in the future.
以后 placed after the subject means 'later' or 'in the future.' After a verb phrase, it means 'after doing X': 吃完以后 = after finishing eating.

5. Translate: "This piece of clothing is a bit long."

► Answer
这件衣服有点长。 🔊
Zhè jiàn yīfu yǒudiǎn cháng。
This piece of clothing is a bit long.
长 (cháng, 2nd tone) = long (in length). Don't confuse with 长 (zhǎng, 3rd tone) = to grow/elder. 有点 = a bit/somewhat, used before adjectives.
