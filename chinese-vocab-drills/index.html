1. 谐音 🔊

► Pinyin
xié yīn
► Answer
A. homophone / pun on similar sounds — 谐音 (xié yīn) refers to words that sound alike, commonly used in Chinese wordplay and puns. Don't confuse with 和音 = harmony.

2. 冬天 🔊

► Pinyin
dōng tiān
► Answer
C. winter — 冬天 (dōng tiān) = winter. Compare: 夏天 (xià tiān) = summer, 春天 = spring, 秋天 = autumn.

3. 古 🔊

► Pinyin
gǔ
► Answer
D. ancient / old — 古 (gǔ) means ancient or old. Used in compounds like 古典 (gǔ diǎn) = classical, 古老 = ancient.

C. 古典 — gǔ diǎn

► Answer
A. 瑜伽 (yú jiā) = yoga. A phonetic loanword. Compare 功夫 = kung fu, 古典 = classical.

► Pinyin
A. 古 — gǔ
B. 市场 — shì chǎng
C. 古典 — gǔ diǎn
D. 谐音 — xié yīn

C. 古典 (gǔ diǎn) = classical. 古 alone = ancient. 古典音乐 = classical music.
Section 3: Fill in the Blank

1. 他从小就学______，现在武功很好。 🔊

► Pinyin
Tā cóng xiǎo jiù xué ______，xiàn zài wǔ gōng hěn hǎo。
► Answer
B. 功夫 — 他从小就学功夫，现在武功很好。
Tā cóng xiǎo jiù xué gōng fu，xiàn zài wǔ gōng hěn hǎo。
He has practiced kung fu since childhood; now his martial arts are very good. — 从小 = since childhood. 武功 = martial arts skill. 功夫 is the discipline/practice itself.

2. 夏天去______买新鲜水果很方便。 🔊

► Pinyin
Xià tiān qù ______ mǎi xīn xiān shuǐ guǒ hěn fāng biàn。
► Answer
C. 市场 — 夏天去市场买新鲜水果很方便。
Xià tiān qù shì chǎng mǎi xīn xiān shuǐ guǒ hěn fāng biàn。
In summer, going to the market to buy fresh fruit is very convenient. — 市场 = open market. 超市 = supermarket. 新鲜 = fresh. Both are plausible but 市场 is the target.
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

1. Translate: "The basketball hoop is quite high — beginners find it difficult."

► Answer
篮球筐很高，初学者觉得很难。 🔊
Lán qiú kuāng hěn gāo，chū xué zhě jué de hěn nán。
The basketball hoop is quite high; beginners find it difficult.
篮球筐 = basketball hoop/basket. 初学者 = beginner (literally 'first-study person'). 觉得 = to feel/think.

2. Translate: "On weekends I like to go to the zoo with my friends."

► Answer
周末我喜欢和朋友去动物园。 🔊
Zhōu mò wǒ xǐ huān hé péng you qù dòng wù yuán。
On weekends I like to go to the zoo with my friends.
动物园 = zoo (lit. 'animal garden'). 和朋友 = with friends. 周末 = weekend.

3. Translate: "In winter, many people like to eat strawberries and mangoes imported from other places."

► Answer
冬天，很多人喜欢吃从外地进口的草莓和芒果。 🔊
Dōng tiān，hěn duō rén xǐ huān chī cóng wài dì jìn kǒu de cǎo méi hé máng guǒ。
In winter, many people like to eat strawberries and mangoes imported from other places.
冬天 sets the time. 草莓 = strawberry, 芒果 = mango. 从外地进口的 = imported from other regions (optional advanced phrase).
