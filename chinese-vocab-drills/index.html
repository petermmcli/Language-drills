Section 1: Chinese → English

Look at the Hanzi. Choose the correct English meaning. Use the Pinyin toggle if you need help.

1. 每个人 🔊

► Pinyin
měi gè rén
► Answer
B. everyone; every person — 每个人 (měi gè rén) literally means “every one person.” Compare 所有人 (suǒyǒurén) which also means “everyone” but emphasizes “all people” collectively.

2. 选择 🔊

► Pinyin
xuǎn zé
► Answer
A. to choose; a choice — 选择 (xuǎn zé) works as both a verb (to choose) and a noun (a choice). The single-character form 选 (xuǎn) is more colloquial.

3. 练习 🔊

► Pinyin
liàn xí
► Answer
B. to practice; practice (n.) — 练习 (liàn xí) covers both the verb and noun. Don’t confuse with 复习 (fùxí) = to review/revise for an exam.

4. 必须 🔊

► Pinyin
bì xū
► Answer
C. must; have to — 必须 (bì xū) expresses strong obligation. Option A, 一定 (yīdìng) = certainly/definitely, has a related but distinct meaning — it can mean “definitely” (assertion) whereas 必须 specifically means “must/have to.”

5. 前 🔊

► Pinyin
qián
► Answer
C. front; before; forward — 前 (qián) appears in many compounds: 前面 (in front), 以前 (before/in the past), 一直往前走 (go straight ahead).
Section 2: English → Chinese

Read the English. Choose the correct Hanzi. Use 🔊 on each option to hear it. Toggle ► Pinyin to reveal readings.

1. attend class; have a lesson

► Pinyin
A. 回答 — huí dá
B. 学 — xué
C. 选择 — xuǎn zé
D. 上课 — shàng kè
► Answer
D. 上课 (shàng kè) — to attend/have class. The opposite is 下课 (xià kè) = class dismissed / to finish class. 上 here means “to go up to / to begin.”

2. to answer; to reply (to a question)

► Pinyin
A. 选 — xuǎn
B. 回答 — huí dá
C. 扶 — fú
D. 错 — cuò
► Answer
B. 回答 (huí dá) — to answer/reply. 回 = return/reply, 答 = answer. Use it in class: 请回答 (please answer). Don’t confuse with 回来 (to come back).

3. to select; to choose (single character, colloquial)

► Pinyin
A. 选 — xuǎn
B. 选择 — xuǎn zé
C. 练习 — liàn xí
D. 一定 — yī dìng
► Answer
A. 选 (xuǎn) — to select / choose (single-character verb). Option B, 选择 (xuǎnzé), is the two-character form meaning “to choose” or “a choice (n.)” — a common exam distractor! 选 alone appears in 选菜 (order dishes) and 选课 (pick a course).

4. to support with one’s hand; to prop up; to help someone steady

► Pinyin
A. 草莓 — cǎo méi
B. 其他 — qí tā
C. 前 — qián
D. 扶 — fú
► Answer
D. 扶 (fú) — to support with one’s hand, to steady or prop up. Common usage: 扶老人 (help an elderly person), 扶梯 (escalator — literally “support stairs”).

5. strawberry

► Pinyin
A. 芒果 — máng guǒ
B. 草莓 — cǎo méi
C. 每个人 — měi gè rén
D. 所有人 — suǒyǒu rén
► Answer
B. 草莓 (cǎo méi) — strawberry. Option A, 芒果 (mángguǒ) = mango — both are fruits, easy to mix up! 草 = grass/plants, 莓 = berry.
Section 3: Fill in the Blank

Choose the word that best completes the sentence. Use 🔊 to hear the full sentence with the correct answer filled in.

1. 我想______中文。 🔊

► Pinyin
Wǒ xiǎng ______ Zhōngwén。
► Answer
A. 学 — 我想学中文。
Wǒ xiǎng xué Zhōngwén。
I want to learn Chinese. — 学 (xué) = to learn. Option B, 练习, means “to practice” something you’re already studying — different nuance. 我想练习中文 = I want to practice Chinese.

2. 老板，可以帮我______吗？ 🔊

► Pinyin
Lǎobǎn，kěyǒ bāng wǒ ______ ma？
► Answer
C. 抹零 — 老板，可以帮我抹零吗？
Lǎobǎn，kěyǒ bāng wǒ mǒ líng ma？
Boss, can you round down for me? — 抹零 (mǒ líng) literally means “wipe away the zeros” — used at markets to ask the seller to waive small change and round down. E.g. if the total is 9.8元, asking 抹零 means you pay just 9元.

3. 古______音乐非常好听。 🔊

► Pinyin
Gǔ ______ yīnyuè fēicháng hǎotīng。
► Answer
B. 典 — 古典音乐非常好听。
Gǔ diǎn yīnyuè fēicháng hǎotīng。
Classical music sounds beautiful. — 典 (diǎn) forms 古典 (gǔdiǎn) = classical. It also appears in 词典 (cídiǎn) = dictionary and 典型 (diǎnxíng) = typical.

4. 孩子，你要______，不能总是说不。 🔊

► Pinyin
Háizi，nǐ yào ______，bù néng zǒngshì shuō bù。
► Answer
C. 听话 — 孩子，你要听话，不能总是说不。
Háizi，nǐ yào tīnghuà，bù néng zǒngshì shuō bù。
Child, you need to be obedient — you can’t always say no. — 听话 (tīnghuà) literally means “listen to words,” but idiomatically means “obedient” or “do as told.” Commonly said to children and pets.

5. ______都应该好好练习中文。 🔊

► Pinyin
______ dōu yīnggāi hǎohǎo liànxí Zhōngwén。
► Answer
B. 所有人 — 所有人都应该好好练习中文。
Suǒyǒu rén dōu yīnggāi hǎohǎo liànxí Zhōngwén。
Everyone should practice Chinese well. — 所有人 (suǒyǒurén) = all people/everyone, emphasizing the whole group. Compare 每个人 (měi gè rén) = every single person (emphasizing each individual).
Section 4: English → Chinese Sentence Translation

Read the English sentence. Write the Chinese translation on paper or in your head, then toggle the answer. Use 🔊 next to the Chinese answer to hear it.

1. Translate: "I think I made a mistake (in what I said)."

► Answer
我觉得我说错了。 🔊
Wǒ juéde wǒ shuō cuò le。
I think I made a mistake in what I said.
错 (cuò) as a resultative complement follows the verb: 说错 = said it wrong, 写错 = wrote it wrong, 走错 = went the wrong way.

2. Translate: "Today we have three class periods."

► Answer
今天我们有三节课。 🔊
Jīntiān wǒmen yǒu sān jié kè。
Today we have three class periods.
节 (jié) is the measure word for class periods: 一节课 = one class period. It also means section, joint, or festival (e.g. 节日 = holiday/festival).

3. Translate: "Do you have any other colors?"

► Answer
你们有其他颜色的吗？ 🔊
Nǐmen yǒu qítā yánsè de ma？
Do you have any other colors?
其他 (qítā) = other/others. As a modifier: 其他颜色 = other colors. As a pronoun: 其他的都可以 = the others are all fine.

4. Translate: "I really like eating mangoes."

► Answer
我非常喜欢吃芒果。 🔊
Wǒ fēicháng xǐhuan chī mángguǒ。
I really like eating mangoes.
芒果 (mángguǒ) = mango. The character 果 (guǒ) means fruit and appears in many fruit names: 苹果 (apple), 草莓 (strawberry — uses 莓 instead). Don’t confuse 芒 with 忙 (busy)!

5. Translate: "You definitely have to practice every day."

► Answer
你一定要每天练习。 🔊
Nǐ yīdìng yào měitiān liànxí。
You definitely have to practice every day.
一定 (yīdìng) = definitely/certainly. 一定要 = must definitely (strong intent or instruction). Compare 必须 (bìxū) which is stronger obligation — 必须 is more like a rule, 一定要 is more personal determination.
