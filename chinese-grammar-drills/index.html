一、Fill-in-the-Blank 填空

Choose the correct word to complete each sentence. Focus on 再 vs 就 (next step vs. reassurance), 先……再……, time expressions with 以前/以后, and experiential 过.

1.你先等一下，我 来。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Nǐ xiān děng yī xià , wǒ ___ lái 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 就
你先等一下，我就来。🔊 Nǐ xiān děng yī xià , wǒ jiù lái 。
Wait a moment, I'll be right there.
再 vs 就 — Next step vs. immediate reassurance

2.我们先吃饭， 去逛商店吧。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ men xiān chī fàn , ___ qù guàng shāng diàn ba 。
▸ Show Answer
B. 再
我们先吃饭，再去逛商店吧。🔊 Wǒ men xiān chī fàn , zài qù guàng shāng diàn ba 。
Let's eat first, then go shopping.
先……再…… — First… then… (sequencing steps)

3.睡觉 ，你要喝点儿热水。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Shuì jiào ___ , nǐ yào hē diǎnr rè shuǐ 。
▸ Show Answer
C. 以前
睡觉以前，你要喝点儿热水。🔊 Shuì jiào yǐ qián , nǐ yào hē diǎnr rè shuǐ 。
Before sleeping, you should drink a little hot water.
Verb/Time + 以前/以后 — Before / After

4.下课 ，我想去喝咖啡。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin

▸ Show Answer
D. 以后

After class, I want to go drink coffee.
Verb/Time + 以前/以后 — Before / After

5.你喝 绿茶吗？ 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin

▸ Show Answer
B. 过
你喝过绿茶吗？🔊 Nǐ hē guò lǜ chá ma ？

三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence has one grammar error. Identify it and write the corrected sentence.

1.Find the error: 你等一下，我再来。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Nǐ děng yī xià , wǒ zài lái 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 再 here means 'come again / come another time.' For immediate reassurance ('I'll be right there'), use 就: 我就来。
Correct:
你等一下，我就来。🔊 Nǐ děng yī xià , wǒ jiù lái 。
Wait a moment, I'll be right there.
再 vs 就 — Next step vs. immediate reassurance

2.Find the error: 我想再复习，先做作业。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ xiǎng zài fù xí , xiān zuò zuò yè 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 先 must introduce the first action, and 再 must introduce the second. The correct order is: 先 + first action + 再 + second action.
Correct:
我想先复习，再做作业。🔊 Wǒ xiǎng xiān fù xí , zài zuò zuò yè 。
I want to first review, then do my homework.
先……再…… — First… then… (sequencing steps)

3.Find the error: 睡觉以后，你不要喝咖啡。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Shuì jiào yǐ hòu , nǐ bù yào hē kā fēi 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 以后 means 'after sleeping' — but the advice is about what to avoid before bed. Use 以前 (before sleeping).
Correct:
睡觉以前，你不要喝咖啡。🔊 Shuì jiào yǐ qián , nǐ bù yào hē kā fēi 。
Before sleeping, you should not drink coffee.
Verb/Time + 以前/以后 — Before / After

4.Find the error: 我没吃了火锅。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ méi chī le huǒ guō 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: To express never having experienced something, use 没 + verb + 过. You cannot combine 没 with 了; replace 了 with 过.
Correct:
我没吃过火锅。🔊 Wǒ méi chī guò huǒ guō 。
I have never eaten hot pot.
