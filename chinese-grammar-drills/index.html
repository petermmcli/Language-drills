Fill-in-the-Blank Translation Error Correction

一、Fill-in-the-Blank 填空

Choose the correct word to complete each sentence.

1.妈妈 妹妹洗衣服。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Mā ma ___ mèi mei xǐ yī fu 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 帮
妈妈帮妹妹洗衣服。🔊 Mā ma bāng mèi mei xǐ yī fu 。
Mom helps younger sister wash her clothes.
帮 + Person + Verb — "Help Someone Do X"

2.服务员 您一百块。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Fú wù yuán ___ nín yī bǎi kuài 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 找
服务员找您一百块。🔊 Fú wù yuán zhǎo nín yī bǎi kuài 。
The server gives you one hundred yuan in change.
找 + Person + Amount — Giving Back Change

3.今天 冷。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Jīn tiān ___ lěng 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 有点儿
今天有点儿冷。🔊 Jīn tiān yǒu diǎnr lěng 。
Today is a bit cold.
一点儿 vs 有点儿 — "A Bit" (Comparative vs Complaint)

4.妹妹 看书呢。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Mèi mei ___ kàn shū ne 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 在
妹妹在看书呢。🔊 Mèi mei zài kàn shū ne 。
Younger sister is reading right now.
G085 — 正在/在 + verb (+ 呢) — Progressive Aspect

三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence contains a grammar error. Find and correct it.

1.Find the error: 朋友帮买东西妹妹。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Péng you bāng mǎi dōng xi mèi mei 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: The person being helped must come directly after 帮, before the verb. Correct order: 帮 + person + verb (+ object).
Correct:
朋友帮妹妹买东西。🔊 Péng you bāng mèi mei mǎi dōng xi 。
A friend helps younger sister buy things.
帮 + Person + Verb — "Help Someone Do X"

2.Find the error: 服务员找一百块给您。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Fú wù yuán zhǎo yī bǎi kuài gěi nín 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: For giving change, the person (indirect object) comes directly after 找: 找 + person + amount. The amount should not be placed before 给.
Correct:
服务员找您一百块。🔊 Fú wù yuán zhǎo nín yī bǎi kuài 。
The server gives you one hundred yuan in change.
找 + Person + Amount — Giving Back Change

3.Find the error: 这个作业一点儿难。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Zhè ge zuò yè yī diǎnr nán 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: When expressing a mild complaint (a bit + adjective), use 有点儿 before the adjective. 一点儿 goes after an adjective for comparison (e.g., 难一点儿 = slightly harder), not before it as a standalone intensifier.
Correct:
这个作业有点儿难。🔊 Zhè ge zuò yè yǒu diǎnr nán 。
This assignment is a bit difficult.
一点儿 vs 有点儿 — "A Bit" (Comparative vs Complaint)

4.Find the error: 妈妈在工作了。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Mā ma zài gōng zuò le 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 了 signals completion or a change of state, which conflicts with the ongoing progressive meaning of 在. For a currently ongoing action, use 呢 (or no particle) instead of 了.
Correct:
妈妈在工作呢。🔊 Mā ma zài gōng zuò ne 。
Mom is working right now.
G085 — 正在/在 + verb (+ 呢) — Progressive Aspect
