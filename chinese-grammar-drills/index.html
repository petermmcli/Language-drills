一、Fill-in-the-Blank 填空

Choose the correct word to complete each sentence.

1.妹妹回家 给我发信息。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Mèi mei huí jiā ___ gěi wǒ fā xìn xi 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 就
妹妹回家就给我发信息。🔊 Mèi mei huí jiā jiù gěi wǒ fā xìn xi 。
As soon as younger sister gets home, she'll send me a message.
再 vs 就 — Next step vs. immediate reassurance

2.先吃饭， 去休息。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Xiān chī fàn ， ___ qù xiū xi 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 再
先吃饭，再去休息。🔊 Xiān chī fàn ， zài qù xiū xi 。
Eat first, then go rest.
先……再…… — First… then… (sequencing steps)

3.下课 ，我去买东西。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Xià kè ___ ， wǒ qù mǎi dōng xi 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 以后
下课以后，我去买东西。🔊 Xià kè yǐ hòu ， wǒ qù mǎi dōng xi 。
After class ends, I'll go buy things.
Verb/Time + 以前/以后 — Before / After

4.哥哥跑 很快。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Gē ge pǎo ___ hěn kuài 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 得
哥哥跑得很快。🔊 Gē ge pǎo de hěn kuài 。
Older brother runs very fast.

三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence contains one error. Identify and correct it.

1.Find the error: 到了家再给妹妹发信息。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Dào le jiā zài gěi mèi mei fā xìn xi 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: When arriving somewhere triggers an immediate result, use 就, not 再. 再 implies doing something again or after a delay; 就 expresses the immediate consequence.
Correct:
到了家就给妹妹发信息。🔊 Dào le jiā jiù gěi mèi mei fā xìn xi 。
As soon as you get home, send your younger sister a message.
再 vs 就 — Next step vs. immediate reassurance

2.Find the error: 再休息，先吃饭。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Zài xiū xi ， xiān chī fàn 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 先 must mark the first action and 再 must mark the second. The structure is always 先 [first action]，再 [second action] — never reversed.
Correct:
先休息，再吃饭。🔊 Xiān xiū xi ， zài chī fàn 。
Rest first, then eat.
先……再…… — First… then… (sequencing steps)

3.Find the error: 吃饭以前了，我洗手。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Chī fàn yǐ qián le ， wǒ xǐ shǒu 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 以前 and 以后 do not take 了. Remove 了 — time markers like 以前 and 以后 are not verbs and cannot carry aspect particles.
Correct:
吃饭以前，我洗手。🔊 Chī fàn yǐ qián ， wǒ xǐ shǒu 。
Before eating, I wash my hands.
Verb/Time + 以前/以后 — Before / After

4.Find the error: 他说得很好中文。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Tā shuō de hěn hǎo zhōng wén 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: When the verb has an object, the object comes before the verb, then the verb is repeated before 得: 中文说得很好. The object cannot follow the degree complement.
Correct:
他中文说得很好。🔊 Tā zhōng wén shuō de hěn hǎo 。
He speaks Chinese very well.
