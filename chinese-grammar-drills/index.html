G133 — 把 Construction — Disposal/Result Frame

三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence contains a grammar error. Identify and correct it.

1.Find the error: 今天冷多了比昨天。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Jīn tiān lěng duō le bǐ zuó tiān 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 比 + B must come directly after the subject (A), before the adjective. The structure is A + 比 + B + adjective + 多了 — not adjective first.
Correct:
今天比昨天冷多了。🔊 Jīn tiān bǐ zuó tiān lěng duō le 。
Today is much colder than yesterday.
Stronger 比 comparison — A + 比 + B + adjective + 多了

2.Find the error: 哥哥不会太游泳。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Gēge bú huì tài yóu yǒng 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: The fixed expression is 不太会, not 不会太. The adverb 太 modifies 会, so 不太 must come together before 会.
Correct:
哥哥不太会游泳。🔊 Gēge bú tài huì yóu yǒng 。
My older brother is not very good at swimming.
很会 / 不太会 + Verb — expressing skill or lack of skill at an action

3.Find the error: 这件衣服一百块比那件贵。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Zhè jiàn yī fu yī bǎi kuài bǐ nà jiàn guì 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: The quantity phrase (the amount of difference) goes after the adjective, at the end: A + 比 + B + adjective + measure phrase. It cannot come before 比.
Correct:
这件衣服比那件贵一百块。🔊 Zhè jiàn yī fu bǐ nà jiàn guì yī bǎi kuài 。
This item of clothing is 100 yuan more expensive than that one.
比 comparison with a quantity — A + 比 + B + adjective + measure phrase

4.Find the error: 妈妈做完了把饭。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Māma zuò wán le bǎ fàn 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: In the 把 construction, 把 + Object must come before the verb, not after it. The correct word order is: Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Result.
Correct:
妈妈把饭做完了。🔊 Māma bǎ fàn zuò wán le 。
Mom finished cooking the food.
