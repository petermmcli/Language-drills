一、Fill-in-the-Blank 填空

Choose the word that best completes each sentence.

1.请你把书 在桌子上。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Qǐng nǐ bǎ shū ___ zài zhuō zi shàng 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 放
请你把书放在桌子上。🔊 Qǐng nǐ bǎ shū fàng zài zhuō zi shàng 。
Please put the book on the table.
把 Construction — Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Result

2.你去 北京吗？ 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Nǐ qù ___ Běi jīng ma ？
▸ Show Answer
B. 过
你去过北京吗？🔊 Nǐ qù guò Běi jīng ma ？
Have you ever been to Beijing?
过 (guò) — Experiential Aspect

3. 天早上我都去运动。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
___ tiān zǎo shàng wǒ dōu qù yùn dòng 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 每
每天早上我都去运动。🔊 Měi tiān zǎo shàng wǒ dōu qù yùn dòng 。
Every morning I exercise without fail.
每…都… — Every … Without Exception

4.我下课 回家。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ xià kè ___ huí jiā 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 就
我下课就回家。🔊 Wǒ xià kè jiù huí jiā 。
As soon as class ends, I go straight home.
G104 — 就 — then / as soon as (sequencing)

三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence contains a grammar mistake. Find and correct it.

1.Find the error: 我做完了把作业。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ zuò wán le bǎ zuò yè 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: In the 把 construction, 把 + object must come directly after the subject, before the verb phrase. The object cannot follow the completed action.
Correct:
我把作业做完了。🔊 Wǒ bǎ zuò yè zuò wán le 。
I finished my homework.
把 Construction — Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Result

2.Find the error: 我有去过上海。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ yǒu qù guò Shàng hǎi 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: In Mandarin, 有 is not inserted before a verb to express past experience. Use verb + 过 directly.
Correct:
我去过上海。🔊 Wǒ qù guò Shàng hǎi 。
I have been to Shanghai before.
过 (guò) — Experiential Aspect

3.Find the error: 他每天喝了咖啡。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Tā měi tiān hē le kā fēi 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: Habitual actions with 每…都… describe a recurring pattern, not a single completed event. Drop 了 and add 都 after the time word to signal 'every time without exception.'
Correct:
他每天都喝咖啡。🔊 Tā měi tiān dōu hē kā fēi 。
He drinks coffee every day.
每…都… — Every … Without Exception

4.Find the error: 她到家，然后她看电视了。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Tā dào jiā ， rán hòu tā kàn diàn shì le 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 就 naturally expresses immediate sequence — 'right after / as soon as.' The 然后 construction is grammatically possible but misses the immediacy and sounds unnatural for a rapid chain of events.
Correct:
她到家就看电视了。🔊 Tā dào jiā jiù kàn diàn shì le 。
As soon as she got home, she watched TV.
G104 — 就 — then / as soon as (sequencing)
