三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence contains one grammatical error. Identify it and write the corrected version.

1.Find the error: 她戴了耳机。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Tā dài le ěr jī 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 了 marks a completed action. For an ongoing or persistent state (e.g. still wearing right now), use V + 着 instead.
Correct:
她戴着耳机。🔊 Tā dài zhe ěr jī 。
She is wearing headphones.
着 (zhe) — persistent state (V + 着)

2.Find the error: 你忙吗不忙？🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Nǐ máng ma bù máng ？
▸ Show Answer
Error: A-not-A questions do not include 吗. Put 不 directly between two copies of the adjective or verb: A + 不 + A.
Correct:
你忙不忙？🔊 Nǐ máng bù máng ？
Are you busy?
Affirmative-negative questions (A 不 A)

3.Find the error: 这件衣服三十五元八角。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Zhè jiàn yī fú sān shí wǔ yuán bā jiǎo 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: In spoken Chinese, use 块 (not 元) for yuan and 毛 (not 角) for jiao. 元 and 角 are formal or written forms.
Correct:
这件衣服三十五块八毛。🔊 Zhè jiàn yī fú sān shí wǔ kuài bā máo 。
This piece of clothing costs 35.80 yuan.
Prices in spoken Chinese — 块 / 毛 system

4.Find the error: 我放书在桌子上。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ fàng shū zài zhuō zi shàng 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: When deliberately placing an object somewhere, use 把 to front the object: Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Location.
Correct:
我把书放在桌子上。🔊 Wǒ bǎ shū fàng zài zhuō zi shàng 。
I put the book on the table.
G133 — 把 Construction — Disposal/Result Frame

5.Find the error: 你喜欢中国菜吗不喜欢？🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Nǐ xǐ huan Zhōng guó cài ma bù xǐ huan ？
▸ Show Answer
Error: In A-not-A questions, the object goes at the end, not between the two verb copies. Use: V + 不 + V + Object.
Correct:
你喜欢不喜欢中国菜？🔊 Nǐ xǐ huan bù xǐ huan Zhōng guó cài ？
Do you like Chinese food?
Affirmative-negative questions (A 不 A)
