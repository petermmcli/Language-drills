Fill-in-the-Blank Translation Error Correction

一、Fill-in-the-Blank 填空

Fill in the blank with the correct word or phrase. Pay attention to whether the action is immediate (就) or sequential (再/先).

1.你先休息一下，我 来帮你。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Nǐ xiān xiūxi yīxià ， wǒ ___ lái bāng nǐ 。
▸ Show Answer
B. 就
你先休息一下，我就来帮你。🔊 Nǐ xiān xiūxi yīxià ， wǒ jiù lái bāng nǐ 。
Just take a rest — I'll come help you right away.
再 vs 就 — Next step vs. immediate reassurance

2.你 写完作业，再看电视。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Nǐ ___ xiě wán zuòyè ， zài kàn diànshì 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 先
你先写完作业，再看电视。🔊 Nǐ xiān xiě wán zuòyè ， zài kàn diànshì 。
First finish your homework, then watch TV.
先……再…… — First… then… (sequencing steps)

3.我每天早上起床 ，先喝一杯水。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang qǐchuáng ___ ， xiān hē yī bēi shuǐ 。
▸ Show Answer
B. 以后
我每天早上起床以后，先喝一杯水。🔊 Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang qǐchuáng yǐhòu ， xiān hē yī bēi shuǐ 。
Every morning after getting up, I drink a glass of water first.
Verb/Time + 以前/以后 — Before / After

三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence contains an error. Identify what is wrong and write the correct version.

1.Find the error: 你去了上海吗？🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Nǐ qù le Shànghǎi ma ？
▸ Show Answer
Error: 了 marks a completed past event but cannot ask about life experience. Use 过 to ask if someone has ever done something.
Correct:
你去过上海吗？🔊 Nǐ qù guò Shànghǎi ma ？
Have you ever been to Shanghai?
G134 — 过 (guò) — Experiential Aspect

2.Find the error: 好，我再做饭。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Hǎo ， wǒ zài zuòfàn 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 再 implies a future or repeated action, not immediacy. 就 conveys 'right away / immediately' as a reassurance.
Correct:
好，我就做饭。🔊 Hǎo ， wǒ jiù zuòfàn 。
Okay, I'll make dinner right away.
再 vs 就 — Next step vs. immediate reassurance

3.Find the error: 吃饭以后，要先洗手。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Chī fàn yǐhòu ， yào xiān xǐ shǒu 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 以后 means 'after.' Washing hands should happen before eating, so 以前 ('before') is correct here.
Correct:
吃饭以前，要先洗手。🔊 Chī fàn yǐqián ， yào xiān xǐ shǒu 。
You should wash your hands before eating.
Verb/Time + 以前/以后 — Before / After
