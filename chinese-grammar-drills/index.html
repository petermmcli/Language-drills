三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence below contains a grammar error. Identify the mistake and write the corrected sentence.

1.Find the error: 孩子跑去进了。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Hái zi pǎo qù jìn le 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: Directional complements follow the main verb: 跑进去 (verb + direction), not 跑去进 (wrong order).
Correct:
孩子跑进去了。🔊 Hái zi pǎo jìn qù le 。
The child ran inside.
进去 / 进来 — V + 去/来 Directional Complement

2.Find the error: 这里的菜很好吃了。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Zhè lǐ de cài hěn hǎo chī le 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 了 after an adjective signals change of state (e.g. 天气热了 = it got hot). For a plain description with 很, remove 了: 很好吃 alone is correct.
Correct:
这里的菜很好吃。🔊 Zhè lǐ de cài hěn hǎo chī 。
The food here is delicious.
Adjective + 了 — Change of State

3.Find the error: 朋友一起和妹妹去了公园。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Péng yǒu yī qǐ hé mèi mei qù le gōng yuán 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: The structure is A + 和 + B + 一起 + verb. 一起 must follow the second person (B), not precede 和: 朋友和妹妹一起去了公园。
Correct:
朋友和妹妹一起去了公园。🔊 Péng yǒu hé mèi mei yī qǐ qù le gōng yuán 。
My friend and younger sister went to the park together.
A + 和 + B + 一起 — "A and B Together"

4.Find the error: 请你一下帮我看问题。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Qǐng nǐ yī xià bāng wǒ kàn wèn tí 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 一下 follows the verb it softens, not precedes it. Correct order: 请你帮我看一下问题。
Correct:
请你帮我看一下问题。🔊 Qǐng nǐ bāng wǒ kàn yī xià wèn tí 。
Please help me look at the problem.
G024 — Soften a request: verb + 一下 (briefly / just a bit)
