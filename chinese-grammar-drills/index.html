一、Fill-in-the-Blank 填空

Choose the word or phrase that best completes each sentence.

1.我妹妹 游泳，她游得很快。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ mèi mei ___ yóu yǒng ， tā yóu de hěn kuài 。
▸ Show Answer
B. 很会
我妹妹很会游泳，她游得很快。🔊 Wǒ mèi mei hěn huì yóu yǒng ， tā yóu de hěn kuài 。
My younger sister is very good at swimming; she swims very fast.
很会 / 不太会 + Verb — expressing skill or lack of skill at an action

2.今天比昨天冷 。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin

▸ Show Answer
C. 多了
今天比昨天冷多了。🔊 Jīn tiān bǐ zuó tiān lěng duō le 。
Today is much colder than yesterday.
Stronger 比 comparison — A + 比 + B + adjective + 多了

3.他明天去北京，我去 他。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin

▸ Show Answer

送 used alone — seeing someone off

4.你去 上海吗？ 🔊

三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence contains one grammar error. Identify it and write the corrected sentence.

1.Find the error: 他很会踢足球了。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Tā hěn huì tī zú qiú le 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 很会 expresses a stable skill or ability — it is not a change of state, so 了 does not belong at the end.
Correct:
他很会踢足球。🔊 Tā hěn huì tī zú qiú 。
He is very good at playing soccer.
很会 / 不太会 + Verb — expressing skill or lack of skill at an action

2.Find the error: 今天比昨天多了冷。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Jīn tiān bǐ zuó tiān duō le lěng 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 多了 must follow the adjective, not precede it. The correct pattern is: A + 比 + B + adjective + 多了.
Correct:
今天比昨天冷多了。🔊 Jīn tiān bǐ zuó tiān lěng duō le 。
Today is much colder than yesterday.
Stronger 比 comparison — A + 比 + B + adjective + 多了

3.Find the error: 我去了送朋友。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ qù le sòng péng yǒu 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: When 去 introduces the purpose verb 送, the completion marker 了 goes at the end of the sentence, not between 去 and 送.
Correct:
我去送朋友了。🔊 Wǒ qù sòng péng yǒu le 。
I went to see my friend off.
送 used alone — seeing someone off

4.Find the error: 我有去过北京。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ yǒu qù guò Běi jīng 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: Standard Mandarin does not use 有 before the verb in experiential sentences. Just use verb + 过 to express past experience.
Correct:
我去过北京。🔊 Wǒ qù guò Běi jīng 。
I have been to Beijing.
