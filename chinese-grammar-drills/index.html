一、Fill-in-the-Blank 填空

Choose the correct word to complete each sentence. Each blank tests a grammar pattern from this week's lesson.

1.妈妈 手机放好了。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Māma ___ shǒu jī fàng hǎo le 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 把
妈妈把手机放好了。🔊 Māma bǎ shǒu jī fàng hǎo le 。
Mom put her phone away properly.
把 Construction — Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Result

2.你吃 火锅吗？ 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Nǐ chī ___ huǒ guō ma ？
▸ Show Answer
A. 过
你吃过火锅吗？🔊 Nǐ chī guò huǒ guō ma ？
Have you ever eaten hot pot?
过 (guò) — Experiential Aspect

3.我是坐出租车来 。 🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ shì zuò chū zū chē lái ___ 。
▸ Show Answer
A. 的
我是坐出租车来的。🔊 Wǒ shì zuò chū zū chē lái de 。
I came by taxi (emphasizing the means of travel).
是…的 Construction — Emphasizing Circumstances of a Past Action

三、Error Correction 改错

Each sentence contains a grammar error. Identify the mistake and write the corrected sentence.

1.Find the error: 我把书看了。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ bǎ shū kàn le 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: The 把 construction requires a result complement after the verb — 了 alone only marks completion but doesn't show what was achieved. Add 完 to indicate the book was fully read.
Correct:
我把书看完了。🔊 Wǒ bǎ shū kàn wán le 。
I finished reading the book.
把 Construction — Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Result

2.Find the error: 我去了上海，所以会说上海话。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Wǒ qù le Shàng hǎi ， suǒ yǐ huì shuō Shàng hǎi huà 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: When describing a past life experience (having been somewhere), use Verb + 过, not Verb + 了. 了 marks a completed action in a specific context; 过 marks a general life experience.
Correct:
我去过上海，所以会说上海话。🔊 Wǒ qù guò Shàng hǎi ， suǒ yǐ huì shuō Shàng hǎi huà 。
I have been to Shanghai, so I can speak Shanghainese.
过 (guò) — Experiential Aspect

3.Find the error: 这个礼物是爸爸昨天买。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Zhè ge lǐ wù shì bà ba zuó tiān mǎi 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: The 是…的 construction must close with 的. Without the final 的, the sentence is incomplete and 是 loses its focus-marking function.
Correct:
这个礼物是爸爸昨天买的。🔊 Zhè ge lǐ wù shì bà ba zuó tiān mǎi de 。
This gift was bought by Dad yesterday.
是…的 Construction — Emphasizing Circumstances of a Past Action

4.Find the error: 天气更来更冷了。🔊

▸ Show Pinyin
Tiān qì gèng lái gèng lěng le 。
▸ Show Answer
Error: 更…更… is not a valid Chinese pattern. For 'more and more,' use 越来越 + adjective. For 'the more X, the more Y,' use 越 + Verb + 越 + Adjective.
Correct:
天气越来越冷了。🔊 Tiān qì yuè lái yuè lěng le 。
The weather is getting colder and colder.
G014 — Proportional comparison: 越…越…
