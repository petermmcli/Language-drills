Exercise 1: 我怎么去上班 — How I Get to Work Easy

Short narrative, single speaker. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 他每天几点上班？

▸ Pinyin
Tā měi tiān jǐ diǎn shàng bān？
▸ answer
他早上八点上班。
Tā zǎo shang bā diǎn shàng bān。
He goes to work at 8 in the morning.

2. 他怎么去公司？

▸ Pinyin
Tā zěn me qù gōng sī？
▸ answer
他坐公交车。有时候先坐地铁，再换公交车。
Tā zuò gōng jiāo chē。 Yǒu shí hou xiān zuò dì tiě， zài huàn gōng jiāo chē。
He takes the bus. Sometimes he first takes the subway, then transfers to the bus.

3. 他几点到公司？

▸ Pinyin
Tā jǐ diǎn dào gōng sī？
▸ answer
最后他八点半到公司。
Zuì hòu tā bā diǎn bàn dào gōng sī。
Finally he arrives at the company at 8:30.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
我每天早上八点上班。🔊 我家离公司不远，坐公交车就到了。🔊 公交车很快，二十分钟就到了。🔊 有时候我先坐地铁，再换公交车。🔊 最后我八点半到公司。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Wǒ měi tiān zǎo shang bā diǎn shàng bān。 Wǒ jiā lí gōng sī bù yuǎn， zuò gōng jiāo chē jiù dào le。 Gōng jiāo chē hěn kuài， èr shí fēn zhōng jiù dào le。 Yǒu shí hou wǒ xiān zuò dì tiě， zài huàn gōng jiāo chē。 Zuì hòu wǒ bā diǎn bàn dào gōng sī。
▸ English Transcript
I go to work at 8 in the morning every day. My home isn't far from the company — I take the bus and I'm there. The bus is very fast — twenty minutes and I'm there. Sometimes I first take the subway, then transfer to the bus. Finally I arrive at the company at 8:30.
Exercise 2: 请问怎么走？ — Excuse Me, How Do I Get There? Medium

Two-speaker dialogue. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full dialogue (2 voices)

1. B 想去哪里？

▸ Pinyin
B xiǎng qù nǎ lǐ？
▸ answer
B 想去火车站。
B xiǎng qù huǒ chē zhàn。
B wants to go to the train station.

2. A 说坐几路车？

▸ Pinyin
A shuō zuò jǐ lù chē？
▸ answer
十六路。
Shí liù lù。
Route 16.

3. B 为什么让 A 再说一遍？

▸ Pinyin
B wèi shén me ràng A zài shuō yí biàn？
▸ answer
因为他没听清楚。
Yīn wèi tā méi tīng qīng chu。
Because he didn't hear clearly.

4. 大概要多长时间？

▸ Pinyin
Dà gài yào duō cháng shí jiān？
▸ answer
半个小时就到了。
Bàn ge xiǎo shí jiù dào le。
Half an hour and you'll be there.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
B: 请问，去火车站怎么走？🔊 A: 你可以坐公交车。🔊 B: 坐几路车？🔊 A: 十六路车。🔊 B: 对不起，我没听清楚，请再说一遍。🔊 A: 十六路。你先坐地铁，再换公交车也可以。🔊 B: 大概要多长时间？🔊 A: 半个小时就到了。🔊 B: 谢谢！最后一个问题：火车站附近有餐厅吗？🔊 A: 有很多。你到了就能看见。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
B: Qǐng wèn， qù huǒ chē zhàn zěn me zǒu？ A: Nǐ kě yǐ zuò gōng jiāo chē。 B: Zuò jǐ lù chē？ A: Shí liù lù chē。 B: Duì bu qǐ， wǒ méi tīng qīng chu， qǐng zài shuō yí biàn。 A: Shí liù lù。 Nǐ xiān zuò dì tiě， zài huàn gōng jiāo chē yě kě yǐ。 B: Dà gài yào duō cháng shí jiān？ A: Bàn ge xiǎo shí jiù dào le。 B: Xiè xie！ Zuì hòu yí ge wèn tí： huǒ chē zhàn fù jìn yǒu cān tīng ma？ A: Yǒu hěn duō。 Nǐ dào le jiù néng kàn jiàn。
▸ English Transcript
B: Excuse me, how do I get to the train station? A: You can take the bus. B: Which bus route? A: Bus route 16. B: Sorry, I didn't hear clearly — please say it again. A: Route 16. You can also first take the subway, then transfer to the bus. B: About how long will it take? A: Half an hour and you're there. B: Thanks! One last question: is there a restaurant near the train station? A: Lots of them. You'll see them when you arrive.
Exercise 3: 去朋友的新家 — Going to a Friend's New Home Harder

Longer narrative, single speaker. More complex grammar. Listen carefully.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 他上个星期六去哪里？

▸ Pinyin
Tā shàng ge xīng qī liù qù nǎ lǐ？
▸ answer
他和朋友一起去了一个新的地方。
Tā hé péng yǒu yì qǐ qù le yí ge xīn de dì fāng。
He went to a new place with a friend.

2. 别人告诉他怎么去？

▸ Pinyin
Bié rén gào su tā zěn me qù？
▸ answer
先坐地铁，再换公交车。
Xiān zuò dì tiě， zài huàn gōng jiāo chē。
First take the subway, then transfer to the bus.

3. 他过了几个站下车？

▸ Pinyin
Tā guò le jǐ ge zhàn xià chē？
▸ answer
六个站。
Liù ge zhàn。
Six stops.

4. 为什么让朋友再说一遍？

▸ Pinyin
Wèi shén me ràng péng yǒu zài shuō yí biàn？
▸ answer
因为他没记住地址。
Yīn wèi tā méi jì zhu dì zhǐ。
Because he didn't remember the address.

5. 他最后到的时候，朋友在哪里？

▸ Pinyin
Tā zuì hòu dào de shí hou， péng yǒu zài nǎ lǐ？
▸ answer
她已经在门口等他了。
Tā yǐ jīng zài mén kǒu děng tā le。
She was already waiting for him at the door.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
上个星期六，我和朋友一起去了一个新的地方。🔊 我不知道怎么走，所以我问了别人。🔊 他们告诉我，可以先坐地铁，再换公交车。🔊 我家离地铁站很近，走五分钟就到了。🔊 坐地铁的人很多，我一直看手机。🔊 过了六个站，我下车换公交车。🔊 公交车比地铁慢一点儿，但是我可以看外面。🔊 我看到很多漂亮的地方，我想下次再来。🔊 我朋友说了地址，我没记住，让她再说一遍。🔊 最后我到的时候，她已经在门口等我了。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Shàng ge xīng qī liù， wǒ hé péng yǒu yì qǐ qù le yí ge xīn de dì fāng。 Wǒ bù zhī dào zěn me zǒu， suǒ yǐ wǒ wèn le bié rén。 Tā men gào su wǒ， kě yǐ xiān zuò dì tiě， zài huàn gōng jiāo chē。 Wǒ jiā lí dì tiě zhàn hěn jìn， zǒu wǔ fēn zhōng jiù dào le。 Zuò dì tiě de rén hěn duō， wǒ yì zhí kàn shǒu jī。 Guò le liù ge zhàn， wǒ xià chē huàn gōng jiāo chē。 Gōng jiāo chē bǐ dì tiě màn yì diǎn er， dàn shì wǒ kě yǐ kàn wài mian。 Wǒ kàn dào hěn duō piào liang de dì fāng， wǒ xiǎng xià cì zài lái。 Wǒ péng yǒu shuō le dì zhǐ， wǒ méi jì zhu， ràng tā zài shuō yí biàn。 Zuì hòu wǒ dào de shí hou， tā yǐ jīng zài mén kǒu děng wǒ le。
▸ English Transcript
Last Saturday, my friend and I went to a new place together. I didn't know how to get there, so I asked other people. They told me I could first take the subway, then transfer to the bus. My home is very close to the subway station — five minutes' walk and I was there. There were lots of people on the subway; I looked at my phone the whole time. After six stops, I got off and transferred to the bus. The bus is a little slower than the subway, but I could see outside. I saw many beautiful places — I want to come back next time. My friend said the address, but I didn't remember, so I asked her to say it again. In the end, when I arrived, she was already waiting for me at the door.
