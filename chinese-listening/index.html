Exercise 1: 今天买东西 — Shopping Today Easy

Short narrative, single speaker. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 他刚才去哪里了？

▸ Pinyin
Tā gāng cái qù nǎ lǐ le？
▸ answer
他去商场买东西了。
Tā qù shāng chǎng mǎi dōng xi le。
He went to the shopping mall to buy things.

2. 他买了什么？

▸ Pinyin
Tā mǎi le shén me？
▸ answer
他买了一件衣服，特别漂亮，也很便宜。
Tā mǎi le yī jiàn yī fu， tè bié piāo liàng， yě hěn pián yi。
He bought a piece of clothing — very pretty and also very cheap.

3. 他想知道什么？

▸ Pinyin
Tā xiǎng zhī dào shén me？
▸ answer
他想知道朋友在哪里。
Tā xiǎng zhī dào péng yǒu zài nǎ lǐ。
He wants to know where his friend is.

4. 他回家以后想做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Tā huí jiā yǐ hòu xiǎng zuò shén me？
▸ answer
他准备先做鸡汤，然后休息一下。
Tā zhǔn bèi xiān zuò jī tāng， rán hòu xiū xi yī xià。
He plans to make chicken soup first, then rest for a bit.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
我刚才去商场买东西了。🔊 我买了一件衣服，特别漂亮，也很便宜。🔊 我朋友也去商场了，但是我们没有见面。🔊 我刚才给他发了信息，问他在哪里。🔊 现在我在家，有点儿饿，想做饭。🔊 我准备先做鸡汤，然后休息一下。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Wǒ gāng cái qù shāng chǎng mǎi dōng xi le。 Wǒ mǎi le yī jiàn yī fu， tè bié piāo liàng， yě hěn pián yi。 Wǒ péng yǒu yě qù shāng chǎng le， dàn shì wǒ men méi yǒu jiàn miàn。 Wǒ gāng cái gěi tā fā le xìn xī， wèn tā zài nǎ lǐ。 Xiàn zài wǒ zài jiā， yǒu diǎn er è， xiǎng zuò fàn。 Wǒ zhǔn bèi xiān zuò jī tāng， rán hòu xiū xi yī xià。
▸ English Transcript
I just went to the shopping mall to buy things. I bought a piece of clothing — very pretty and very cheap. My friend also went to the mall, but we didn't meet up. Just now I sent him a message asking where he is. Now I'm home, a little hungry, and want to cook. I plan to make chicken soup first, then rest for a bit.
Exercise 2: 在商场找人 — Finding Someone in the Mall Medium

Two-speaker dialogue. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full dialogue (2 voices)

1. A 刚才在哪里看见 B？

▸ Pinyin
A gāng cái zài nǎ lǐ kàn jiàn B？
▸ answer
A 在西门看见 B 了。
A zài xī mén kàn jiàn B le。
A saw B at the west gate.

2. B 在商场哪边？旁边是什么？

▸ Pinyin
B zài shāng chǎng nǎ biān？ páng biān shì shén me？
▸ answer
B 在右边，旁边是餐厅。
B zài yòu biān， páng biān shì cān tīng。
B is on the right side; next to a restaurant.

3. 怎么走去 B 那里？

▸ Pinyin
Zěn me zǒu qù B nà lǐ？
▸ answer
一直走，然后右拐。
Yī zhí zǒu， rán hòu yòu guǎi。
Go straight, then turn right.

4. A 怎么了？

▸ Pinyin
A zěn me le？
▸ answer
A 有点儿饿了。
A yǒu diǎn er è le。
A is a little hungry.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
A: 你在哪里？我刚才在西门看见你了。🔊 B: 是吗？我刚才也在西门，现在我进商场了。🔊 A: 我也在商场里面！你在哪边？🔊 B: 我在右边，旁边是餐厅。🔊 A: 好，我在左边。你过来这边吧。🔊 B: 好的，等一下，我现在过来。🔊 A: 你知道怎么走吗？🔊 B: 知道，一直走，然后右拐，对吗？🔊 A: 对！快一点儿，我有点儿饿了。🔊 B: 好的，我很快过来！🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
A: Nǐ zài nǎ lǐ？ Wǒ gāng cái zài xī mén kàn jiàn nǐ le。 B: Shì ma？ Wǒ gāng cái yě zài xī mén， xiàn zài wǒ jìn shāng chǎng le。 A: Wǒ yě zài shāng chǎng lǐ miàn！ Nǐ zài nǎ biān？ B: Wǒ zài yòu biān， páng biān shì cān tīng。 A: Hǎo， wǒ zài zuǒ biān。 Nǐ guò lái zhè biān ba。 B: Hǎo de， děng yī xià， wǒ xiàn zài guò lái。 A: Nǐ zhī dào zěn me zǒu ma？ B: Zhī dào， yī zhí zǒu， rán hòu yòu guǎi， duì ma？ A: Duì！ Kuài yī diǎn er， wǒ yǒu diǎn er è le。 B: Hǎo de， wǒ hěn kuài guò lái！
▸ English Transcript
A: Where are you? I just saw you at the west gate! B: Really? I was just at the west gate too — now I've gone inside the mall. A: I'm inside the mall too! Which side are you on? B: I'm on the right side — there's a restaurant next to me. A: OK, I'm on the left. Come over this way. B: Sure, hang on — I'm coming now. A: Do you know how to get here? B: Yes — go straight, then turn right. Is that right? A: Correct! Hurry up a bit — I'm getting a little hungry. B: OK, I'll be there soon!
Exercise 3: 朋友来我家 — A Friend Coming Over Harder

Longer narrative, single speaker. More complex grammar. Listen carefully.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 他朋友什么时候过来？

▸ Pinyin
Tā péng yǒu shén me shí hou guò lái？
▸ answer
他下午过来，现在在路上了。
Tā xià wǔ guò lái， xiàn zài zài lù shàng le。
He's coming in the afternoon — he's already on his way.

2. 他要做什么菜？

▸ Pinyin
Tā yào zuò shén me cài？
▸ answer
他要做四川菜，比如鱼肉和牛肉，还有蔬菜。
Tā yào zuò Sì chuān cài， bǐ rú yú ròu hé niú ròu， hái yǒu shū cài。
He wants to make Sichuan food, like fish and beef, plus vegetables.

3. 他的朋友喜欢喝什么？

▸ Pinyin
Tā de péng yǒu xǐ huān hē shén me？
▸ answer
他的朋友喜欢喝茶。
Tā de péng yǒu xǐ huān hē chá。
His friend likes to drink tea.

4. 如果天气好，他们可以做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Rú guǒ tiān qì hǎo， tā men kě yǐ zuò shén me？
▸ answer
他们可以去公园走走。
Tā men kě yǐ qù gōng yuán zǒu zǒu。
They can go for a walk in the park.

5. 对他来说，什么是最开心的事？

▸ Pinyin
Duì tā lái shuō， shén me shì zuì kāi xīn de shì？
▸ answer
朋友来家里是最开心的事。
Péng yǒu lái jiā lǐ shì zuì kāi xīn de shì。
Having friends come home is the happiest thing.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
我朋友今天要来我家吃饭，我很高兴。🔊 刚才我给他打电话，问他几点过来。🔊 他告诉我，他下午过来，现在在路上了。🔊 我需要先去商场买东西，买一些蔬菜和肉。🔊 我要做四川菜，比如鱼肉和牛肉，还有一些蔬菜。🔊 做饭以后不要忘了洗碗。🔊 我朋友很喜欢喝茶，所以我也准备了一些茶。🔊 如果天气好，我们可以去公园走走。🔊 我们经常一起聊天，聊很长时间，特别有趣。🔊 有时候我们聊到很晚，但是我们很开心。🔊 对我来说，朋友来家里是最开心的事。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Wǒ péng yǒu jīn tiān yào lái wǒ jiā chī fàn， wǒ hěn gāo xìng。 Gāng cái wǒ gěi tā dǎ diàn huà， wèn tā jǐ diǎn guò lái。 Tā gào su wǒ， tā xià wǔ guò lái， xiàn zài zài lù shàng le。 Wǒ xū yào xiān qù shāng chǎng mǎi dōng xi， mǎi yī xiē shū cài hé ròu。 Wǒ yào zuò Sì chuān cài， bǐ rú yú ròu hé niú ròu， hái yǒu yī xiē shū cài。 Zuò fàn yǐ hòu bù yào wàng le xǐ wǎn。 Wǒ péng yǒu hěn xǐ huān hē chá， suǒ yǐ wǒ yě zhǔn bèi le yī xiē chá。 Rú guǒ tiān qì hǎo， wǒ men kě yǐ qù gōng yuán zǒu zǒu。 Wǒ men jīng cháng yī qǐ liáo tiān， liáo hěn cháng shí jiān， tè bié yǒu qù。 Yǒu shí hou wǒ men liáo dào hěn wǎn， dàn shì wǒ men hěn kāi xīn。 Duì wǒ lái shuō， péng yǒu lái jiā lǐ shì zuì kāi xīn de shì。
▸ English Transcript
My friend is coming to my place to eat today — I'm really happy. Just now I called him to ask what time he's coming. He told me he's coming in the afternoon — he's already on his way. I need to go to the mall first to buy things — some vegetables and meat. I'm going to make Sichuan food, like fish and beef, plus some vegetables. After cooking, don't forget to wash the dishes. My friend really likes tea, so I've also prepared some tea. If the weather is nice, we can go for a walk in the park. We often chat together for a long time — it's especially fun. Sometimes we talk until very late, but we're always very happy. For me, having friends come home is the happiest thing.
