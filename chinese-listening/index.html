Exercise 1: 地板漏水了 — The Floor Is Leaking Easy

Short narrative, single speaker. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 我家怎么了？

▸ Pinyin
Wǒ jiā zěn me le？
▸ answer
我家的地板漏水了。
Wǒ jiā de dì bǎn lòu shuǐ le。
My floor at home is leaking water.

2. 我为什么生气？

▸ Pinyin
Wǒ wèi shén me shēng qì？
▸ answer
因为水太多。
Yīn wèi shuǐ tài duō。
Because there is too much water.

3. 结果怎么样？

▸ Pinyin
Jié guǒ zěn me yàng？
▸ answer
结果不错，现在没有问题了。
Jié guǒ bú cuò， xiàn zài méi yǒu wèn tí le。
The result was good — there's no problem now.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
我家的地板漏水了。🔊 我很生气，因为水太多。🔊 我给房东打电话。🔊 房东说他一会儿来。🔊 然后房东来了，他帮助我。🔊 我们一起看地板。🔊 结果不错，现在没有问题了。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Wǒ jiā de dì bǎn lòu shuǐ le。 Wǒ hěn shēng qì， yīn wèi shuǐ tài duō。 Wǒ gěi fáng dōng dǎ diàn huà。 Fáng dōng shuō tā yī huǐ r lái。 Rán hòu fáng dōng lái le， tā bāng zhù wǒ。 Wǒ men yì qǐ kàn dì bǎn。 Jié guǒ bú cuò， xiàn zài méi yǒu wèn tí le。
▸ English Transcript
My floor at home is leaking water. I'm very angry because there is too much water. I called the landlord. The landlord said he'd come in a little while. Then the landlord came, and he helped me. We looked at the floor together. The result was good — there's no problem now.
Exercise 2: 妈妈出院了 — Mom Left the Hospital Medium

Two-speaker dialogue. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full dialogue (2 voices)

1. 妈妈今天怎么样？

▸ Pinyin
Mā ma jīn tiān zěn me yàng？
▸ answer
妈妈今天出院了。
Mā ma jīn tiān chū yuàn le。
Mom got discharged from the hospital today.

2. 妈妈每天做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Mā ma měi tiān zuò shén me？
▸ answer
每天要吃药两次。
Měi tiān yào chī yào liǎng cì。
She has to take medicine twice every day.

3. 为什么妈妈生气？

▸ Pinyin
Wèi shén me mā ma shēng qì？
▸ answer
因为她不能出去。
Yīn wèi tā bù néng chū qù。
Because she can't go out.

4. 我会做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Wǒ huì zuò shén me？
▸ answer
我会每天照顾妈妈。
Wǒ huì měi tiān zhào gù mā ma。
I'll take care of Mom every day.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
B: 你妈妈今天怎么样？🔊 A: 我妈妈今天出院了。🔊 B: 太好了！她身体好吗？🔊 A: 还行，但是每天要吃药两次。🔊 B: 她生气吗？🔊 A: 有一点儿，因为她不能出去。🔊 B: 没关系，你可以照顾她。🔊 A: 是的，我会每天照顾我妈妈。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
B: Nǐ mā ma jīn tiān zěn me yàng？ A: Wǒ mā ma jīn tiān chū yuàn le。 B: Tài hǎo le！ Tā shēn tǐ hǎo ma？ A: Hái xíng， dàn shì měi tiān yào chī yào liǎng cì。 B: Tā shēng qì ma？ A: Yǒu yì diǎn r， yīn wèi tā bù néng chū qù。 B: Méi guān xi， nǐ kě yǐ zhào gù tā。 A: Shì de， wǒ huì měi tiān zhào gù wǒ mā ma。
▸ English Transcript
B: How is your mom today? A: My mom got discharged from the hospital today. B: That's great! Is she feeling well? A: She's OK, but has to take medicine twice every day. B: Is she angry? A: A little bit, because she can't go out. B: It's OK — you can take care of her. A: Yes, I'll take care of my mom every day.
Exercise 3: 我的中文课 — My Chinese Class Harder

Longer narrative, single speaker. More complex grammar. Listen carefully.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 上个星期我去哪儿？

▸ Pinyin
Shàng ge xīng qī wǒ qù nǎ r？
▸ answer
我和同学一起去学校。
Wǒ hé tóng xué yì qǐ qù xué xiào。
I went to school with classmates.

2. 我的听力怎么样？

▸ Pinyin
Wǒ de tīng lì zěn me yàng？
▸ answer
听力不太好。
Tīng lì bú tài hǎo。
My listening isn't very good.

3. 我不明白的时候，我做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Wǒ bù míng bái de shí hou， wǒ zuò shén me？
▸ answer
我问老师答案。
Wǒ wèn lǎo shī dá àn。
I ask the teacher for the answers.

4. 我以后要做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Wǒ yǐ hòu yào zuò shén me？
▸ answer
每天要读书。
Měi tiān yào dú shū。
I need to read every day.

5. 我喜欢什么？

▸ Pinyin
Wǒ xǐ huan shén me？
▸ answer
我非常喜欢中文。
Wǒ fēi cháng xǐ huan zhōng wén。
I like Chinese very much.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
上个星期，我和同学一起去学校。🔊 我们是中学的学生，老师给我们一些书。🔊 老师说，我们要填写一个东西。🔊 我的阅读水平不错，但是听力不太好。🔊 我看好多书，一些书很有意思。🔊 一些书太难，我不明白。🔊 我问老师答案。🔊 老师帮助我，然后我明白了。🔊 我知道以后每天要读书。🔊 我非常喜欢中文。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Shàng ge xīng qī， wǒ hé tóng xué yì qǐ qù xué xiào。 Wǒ men shì zhōng xué de xué sheng， lǎo shī gěi wǒ men yì xiē shū。 Lǎo shī shuō， wǒ men yào tián xiě yí ge dōng xi。 Wǒ de yuè dú shuǐ píng bú cuò， dàn shì tīng lì bú tài hǎo。 Wǒ kàn hǎo duō shū， yì xiē shū hěn yǒu yì si。 Yì xiē shū tài nán， wǒ bù míng bái。 Wǒ wèn lǎo shī dá àn。 Lǎo shī bāng zhù wǒ， rán hòu wǒ míng bái le。 Wǒ zhī dào yǐ hòu měi tiān yào dú shū。 Wǒ fēi cháng xǐ huan zhōng wén。
▸ English Transcript
Last week, I went to school with classmates. We are middle school students; the teacher gave us some books. The teacher said we needed to fill out a form. My reading level is pretty good, but my listening isn't very good. I read a lot of books; some books are very interesting. Some books are too hard — I don't understand them. I asked the teacher for the answers. The teacher helped me, and then I understood. I know that from now on I need to read every day. I like Chinese very much.
