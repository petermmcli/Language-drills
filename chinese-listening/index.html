Exercise 1: 学校附近 — Near the School Easy

Short narrative, single speaker. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 银行在哪里？

▸ Pinyin
Yín háng zài nǎ lǐ？
▸ answer
银行在学校对面。
Yín háng zài xué xiào duì miàn。
The bank is across from the school.

2. 银行旁边有什么？

▸ Pinyin
Yín háng páng biān yǒu shén me？
▸ answer
旁边有一家咖啡店。
Páng biān yǒu yī jiā kā fēi diàn。
Next to it is a coffee shop.

3. 说话的人经常做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Shuō huà de rén jīng cháng zuò shén me？
▸ answer
经常去公园休息，也喜欢在咖啡店喝咖啡。
Jīng cháng qù gōng yuán xiū xi， yě xǐ huān zài kā fēi diàn hē kā fēi。
Often goes to the park to rest, and likes drinking coffee at the coffee shop.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
我的学校附近有很多东西。🔊 银行就在学校对面，非常方便。🔊 银行旁边有一家咖啡店。🔊 咖啡店左边有一个公园。🔊 公园里很漂亮，有很多树。🔊 我经常去公园休息，也喜欢在咖啡店喝咖啡。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Wǒ de xué xiào fù jìn yǒu hěn duō dōng xi。 Yín háng jiù zài xué xiào duì miàn， fēi cháng fāng biàn。 Yín háng páng biān yǒu yī jiā kā fēi diàn。 Kā fēi diàn zuǒ biān yǒu yī ge gōng yuán。 Gōng yuán lǐ hěn piào liang， yǒu hěn duō shù。 Wǒ jīng cháng qù gōng yuán xiū xi， yě xǐ huān zài kā fēi diàn hē kā fēi。
▸ English Transcript
There are many things near my school. The bank is right across from the school — very convenient. Next to the bank there is a coffee shop. To the left of the coffee shop there is a park. The park is very beautiful and has many trees. I often go to the park to rest, and I also like drinking coffee at the coffee shop.
Exercise 2: 学汉语的建议 — Tips for Learning Chinese Medium

Two-speaker dialogue. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full dialogue (2 voices)

1. B 最近学汉语学得怎么样？

▸ Pinyin
B zuì jìn xué Hàn yǔ xué de zěn me yàng？
▸ answer
还行，但是有一点儿难。
Hái xíng， dàn shì yǒu yī diǎn er nán。
So-so, but a little difficult.

2. A 举的例子是什么？

▸ Pinyin
A jǔ de lì zi shì shén me？
▸ answer
每天早上学一个小时汉字。
Měi tiān zǎo shàng xué yī ge xiǎo shí Hàn zì。
Study Chinese characters for one hour every morning.

3. A 还建议了什么？

▸ Pinyin
A hái jiàn yì le shén me？
▸ answer
经常听中文音乐，很有意思。
Jīng cháng tīng zhōng wén yīn yuè， hěn yǒu yì si。
Often listen to Chinese music — it's very interesting.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
A: 你好！你最近学汉语学得怎么样？🔊 B: 还行，但是有一点儿难。🔊 A: 我可以帮你。我举一个例子吧。🔊 B: 好的，谢谢！🔊 A: 比如，你可以每天早上学一个小时汉字。🔊 B: 这个例子很好！🔊 A: 还有，你要经常听中文音乐，很有意思。🔊 B: 对！音乐很有意思。🔊 A: 你觉得这个道理合理吗？🔊 B: 非常合理！谢谢你！🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
A: Nǐ hǎo！ Nǐ zuì jìn xué Hàn yǔ xué de zěn me yàng？ B: Hái xíng， dàn shì yǒu yī diǎn er nán。 A: Wǒ kě yǐ bāng nǐ。 Wǒ jǔ yī ge lì zi ba。 B: Hǎo de， xiè xie！ A: Bǐ rú， nǐ kě yǐ měi tiān zǎo shàng xué yī ge xiǎo shí Hàn zì。 B: Zhè ge lì zi hěn hǎo！ A: Hái yǒu， nǐ yào jīng cháng tīng zhōng wén yīn yuè， hěn yǒu yì si。 B: Duì！ Yīn yuè hěn yǒu yì si。 A: Nǐ jué de zhè ge dào lǐ hé lǐ ma？ B: Fēi cháng hé lǐ！ Xiè xie nǐ！
▸ English Transcript
A: Hi! How has your Chinese study been going lately? B: So-so, but a little difficult. A: I can help you. Let me give you an example. B: Great, thank you! A: For instance, you could study Chinese characters for one hour every morning. B: That's a great example! A: Also, you should often listen to Chinese music — it's very interesting. B: Right! Music is very interesting. A: Do you think this logic is reasonable? B: Very reasonable! Thank you!
Exercise 3: 我的工作日 — My Working Day Harder

Longer narrative, single speaker. More complex grammar. Listen carefully.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 公司在哪里？

▸ Pinyin
Gōng sī zài nǎ lǐ？
▸ answer
公司就在银行对面。
Gōng sī jiù zài yín háng duì miàn。
The company is right across from the bank.

2. 老板喜欢做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Lǎo bǎn xǐ huān zuò shén me？
▸ answer
老板喜欢举例子。
Lǎo bǎn xǐ huān jǔ lì zi。
The boss likes to give examples.

3. 老板举了什么例子？

▸ Pinyin
Lǎo bǎn jǔ le shén me lì zi？
▸ answer
他说我们要经常学习，不要只工作不学习。
Tā shuō wǒ men yào jīng cháng xué xí， bú yào zhǐ gōng zuò bù xué xí。
He said we should study often, not just work without studying.

4. 晚上，说话的人做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Wǎn shàng， shuō huà de rén zuò shén me？
▸ answer
先吃晚饭，然后看一些电视剧。
Xiān chī wǎn fàn， rán hòu kàn yī xiē diàn shì jù。
First eat dinner, then watch some TV dramas.

5. 睡觉以前，为什么不看手机？

▸ Pinyin
Shuì jiào yǐ qián， wèi shén me bú kàn shǒu jī？
▸ answer
因为对身体不好。
Yīn wèi duì shēn tǐ bù hǎo。
Because it is bad for the body.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
我在一家公司工作，公司就在银行对面。🔊 每天早上，我坐出租车去公司，大概二十分钟。🔊 到了公司，同事站在我面前说你好。🔊 我的老板喜欢举例子，经常举一个工作的例子。🔊 比如，他说我们要经常学习，不要只工作不学习。🔊 我觉得他说的很有道理，也很合理。🔊 中午，我和同事一起在餐厅吃午饭。🔊 下午有时候忙，有时候有时间喝一杯咖啡。🔊 晚上，我先吃晚饭，然后看一些电视剧。🔊 我最喜欢看韩国的电视剧，觉得很有意思。🔊 睡觉以前，我不看手机，因为对身体不好。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Wǒ zài yī jiā gōng sī gōng zuò， gōng sī jiù zài yín háng duì miàn。 Měi tiān zǎo shàng， wǒ zuò chū zū chē qù gōng sī， dà gài èr shí fēn zhōng。 Dào le gōng sī， tóng shì zhàn zài wǒ miàn qián shuō nǐ hǎo。 Wǒ de lǎo bǎn xǐ huān jǔ lì zi， jīng cháng jǔ yī ge gōng zuò de lì zi。 Bǐ rú， tā shuō wǒ men yào jīng cháng xué xí， bú yào zhǐ gōng zuò bù xué xí。 Wǒ jué de tā shuō de hěn yǒu dào lǐ， yě hěn hé lǐ。 Zhōng wǔ， wǒ hé tóng shì yī qǐ zài cān tīng chī wǔ fàn。 Xià wǔ yǒu shí hou máng， yǒu shí hou yǒu shí jiān hē yī bēi kā fēi。 Wǎn shàng， wǒ xiān chī wǎn fàn， rán hòu kàn yī xiē diàn shì jù。 Wǒ zuì xǐ huān kàn Hán guó de diàn shì jù， jué de hěn yǒu yì si。 Shuì jiào yǐ qián， wǒ bú kàn shǒu jī， yīn wèi duì shēn tǐ bú hǎo。
▸ English Transcript
I work at a company — the company is right across from the bank. Every morning I take a taxi to the company, about twenty minutes. When I arrive at the company, colleagues stand in front of me and say hello. My boss likes to give examples — often gives a work-related example. For instance, he says we should study often, not just work without studying. I think what he says makes a lot of sense and is very reasonable. At noon I eat lunch at a restaurant together with colleagues. In the afternoon, sometimes I'm busy; sometimes I have time for a cup of coffee. In the evening, I first eat dinner, then watch some TV dramas. I like watching Korean TV dramas the most — I find them very interesting. Before sleeping, I don't look at my phone, because it's bad for the body.
