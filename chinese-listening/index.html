Exercise 1: 去公园 — Going to the Park Easy

Short narrative, single speaker. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 今天天气怎么样？

▸ Pinyin
Jīn tiān tiān qì zěn me yàng？
▸ answer
今天天气很好。
Jīn tiān tiān qì hěn hǎo。
The weather today is very good.

2. 公园离他家远吗？

▸ Pinyin
Gōng yuán lí tā jiā yuǎn ma？
▸ answer
不远，公园就在他家附近，很近，非常方便。
Bù yuǎn， gōng yuán jiù zài tā jiā fù jìn， hěn jìn， fēi cháng fāng biàn。
Not far — the park is right near his home, very close, very convenient.

3. 公园里有什么？

▸ Pinyin
Gōng yuán lǐ yǒu shén me？
▸ answer
公园里有很多漂亮的花。
Gōng yuán lǐ yǒu hěn duō piào liang de huā。
There are many beautiful flowers in the park.

4. 他在公园里走了多长时间？

▸ Pinyin
Tā zài gōng yuán lǐ zǒu le duō cháng shí jiān？
▸ answer
他走了很长时间。
Tā zǒu le hěn cháng shí jiān。
He walked for a long time.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
今天天气很好。🔊 我想去公园走走。🔊 公园就在我家附近，很近，非常方便。🔊 公园里有很多漂亮的花。🔊 我在公园里走了很长时间。🔊 我真的很开心。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Jīn tiān tiān qì hěn hǎo。 Wǒ xiǎng qù gōng yuán zǒu zou。 Gōng yuán jiù zài wǒ jiā fù jìn， hěn jìn， fēi cháng fāng biàn。 Gōng yuán lǐ yǒu hěn duō piào liang de huā。 Wǒ zài gōng yuán lǐ zǒu le hěn cháng shí jiān。 Wǒ zhēn de hěn kāi xīn。
▸ English Transcript
The weather today is very good. I want to go for a walk in the park. The park is right near my home — very close, very convenient. There are many beautiful flowers in the park. I walked in the park for a long time. I was truly very happy.
Exercise 2: 在商场门口见面 — Meeting at the Mall Entrance Medium

Two-speaker dialogue. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full dialogue (2 voices)

1. A 迷路了吗？她找不到什么？

▸ Pinyin
A mí lù le ma？ Tā zhǎo bù dào shén me？
▸ answer
是的，A 迷路了，找不到路，找不到东门。
Shì de， A mí lù le， zhǎo bù dào dōng mén。
Yes, A got lost and couldn't find the east gate.

2. A 旁边有什么？

▸ Pinyin
A páng biān yǒu shén me？
▸ answer
A 在一个咖啡店旁边，附近有一个公园。
A zài yī ge kā fēi diàn páng biān， fù jìn yǒu yī ge gōng yuán。
A is beside a coffee shop, and there is a park nearby.

3. B 怎么告诉 A 找到东门？

▸ Pinyin
B zěn me gào su A zhǎo dào dōng mén？
▸ answer
走到公园门口，然后直走，就可以看到东门。
Zǒu dào gōng yuán mén kǒu， rán hòu zhí zǒu， jiù kě yǐ kàn dào dōng mén。
Walk to the park entrance, then go straight, and you'll see the east gate.

4. 他们进商场要做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Tā men jìn shāng chǎng yào zuò shén me？
▸ answer
他们要逛一逛，B 要买一些东西。
Tā men yào guàng yi guàng， B yào mǎi yī xiē dōng xi。
They want to stroll around; B wants to buy some things.

5. A 说东门方便吗？

▸ Pinyin
A shuō dōng mén fāng biàn ma？
▸ answer
是的，A 说真的很方便，就在旁边。
Shì de， A shuō zhēn de hěn fāng biàn， jiù zài páng biān。
Yes, A said it was really convenient — right beside her.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
B: 喂，你在哪里？我到商场东门了。🔊
A: 对不起，我迷路了，找不到路，找不到东门。🔊
B: 没事儿。你现在在哪个地方？🔊
A: 我在一个咖啡店旁边，附近有一个公园。🔊
B: 好，我知道了。你走到公园门口，然后直走，就可以看到东门。🔊
A: 谢谢！我已经看到公园了，我走过去。🔊
B: 好呀，我在东门等你。🔊
A: 我到了，我在东门！真的很方便，就在旁边。🔊
B: 太好了！我们进去逛一逛吧，我要买一些东西。🔊
A: 好的，走吧！🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
B: Wèi， nǐ zài nǎ lǐ？ Wǒ dào shāng chǎng dōng mén le。 A: Duì bu qǐ， wǒ mí lù le， zhǎo bù dào lù， zhǎo bù dào dōng mén。 B: Méi shìr。 Nǐ xiàn zài zài nǎ ge dì fang？ A: Wǒ zài yī ge kā fēi diàn páng biān， fù jìn yǒu yī ge gōng yuán。 B: Hǎo， wǒ zhī dào le。 Nǐ zǒu dào gōng yuán mén kǒu， rán hòu zhí zǒu， jiù kě yǐ kàn dào dōng mén。 A: Xiè xie！ Wǒ yǐ jīng kàn dào gōng yuán le， wǒ zǒu guò qù。 B: Hǎo ya， wǒ zài dōng mén děng nǐ。 A: Wǒ dào le， wǒ zài dōng mén！ Zhēn de hěn fāng biàn， jiù zài páng biān。 B: Tài hǎo le！ Wǒ men jìn qù guàng yi guàng ba， wǒ yào mǎi yī xiē dōng xi。 A: Hǎo de， zǒu ba！
▸ English Transcript
B: Hello, where are you? I've arrived at the mall's east gate. A: Sorry, I got lost — I can't find the way, I can't find the east gate. B: No problem. What place are you at right now? A: I'm next to a coffee shop, with a park nearby. B: Got it. Walk to the park entrance, then go straight, and you'll be able to see the east gate. A: Thanks! I can already see the park — I'll walk over. B: Great, I'll wait for you at the east gate. A: I'm here — I'm at the east gate! It's really convenient, right next to me. B: Excellent! Let's go inside and have a browse — I want to buy a few things. A: Sure, let's go!
Exercise 3: 我的一天 — My Day Out Harder

Longer narrative, single speaker. More complex grammar. Listen carefully.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 她早上打电话给谁？要做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Tā zǎo shang dǎ diàn huà gěi shuí？ Yào zuò shén me？
▸ answer
她打电话给朋友，问她有没有时间一起出去逛一逛。
Tā dǎ diàn huà gěi péng yǒu， wèn tā yǒu méi yǒu shí jiān yī qǐ chū qù guàng yi guàng。
She called her friend, asking if she had time to go out for a stroll together.

2. 他们在哪儿见面？为什么她早点出发？

▸ Pinyin
Tā men zài nǎr jiàn miàn？ Wèi shén me tā zǎo diǎn chū fā？
▸ answer
她们在商场西门见面。因为她是路痴，经常迷路，所以早点出发。
Tā men zài shāng chǎng xī mén jiàn miàn。 Yīn wèi tā shì lù chī， jīng cháng mí lù， suǒ yǐ zǎo diǎn chū fā。
They met at the mall's west gate. Because she has a poor sense of direction and often gets lost, she left early.

3. 她在西门附近看到了什么？

▸ Pinyin
Tā zài xī mén fù jìn kàn dào le shén me？
▸ answer
她看到一个漂亮的公园在旁边，就在商场附近，真的很方便。
Tā kàn dào yī ge piào liang de gōng yuán zài páng biān， jiù zài shāng chǎng fù jìn， zhēn de hěn fāng biàn。
She saw a beautiful park next to her, right near the mall — really convenient.

4. 下午她们去吃什么？怎么样？

▸ Pinyin
Xià wǔ tā men qù chī shén me？ Zěn me yàng？
▸ answer
她们去吃川菜，特别好吃。
Tā men qù chī chuān cài， tè bié hǎo chī。
They went to eat Sichuan cuisine — especially delicious.

5. 回家的时候，她给老板发了什么？说了什么？

▸ Pinyin
Huí jiā de shí hou， tā gěi lǎo bǎn fā le shén me？ Shuō le shén me？
▸ answer
她给老板发了一封邮件，告诉他明天她要请假。
Tā gěi lǎo bǎn fā le yī fēng yóu jiàn， gào su tā míng tiān tā yào qǐng jià。
She sent her boss an email, telling him she would take a day off tomorrow.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
今天上午，我打电话给朋友，问她有没有时间一起出去逛一逛。🔊 她说好呀，我们在商场西门见面。🔊 我是一个路痴，经常迷路，所以我早点出发。🔊 走到西门的时候，我看到一个漂亮的公园在旁边，就在商场附近，真的很方便。🔊 我已经找到西门了，但是我的朋友还没到。🔊 我等了几分钟，终于看见她了。🔊 我们进了商场，逛了很多地方，买了一些东西。🔊 下午我们去吃川菜，特别好吃。🔊 回家的时候，我给老板发了一封邮件，告诉他明天我要请假。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Jīn tiān shàng wǔ， wǒ dǎ diàn huà gěi péng yǒu， wèn tā yǒu méi yǒu shí jiān yī qǐ chū qù guàng yi guàng。 Tā shuō hǎo ya， wǒ men zài shāng chǎng xī mén jiàn miàn。 Wǒ shì yī ge lù chī， jīng cháng mí lù， suǒ yǐ wǒ zǎo diǎn chū fā。 Zǒu dào xī mén de shí hou， wǒ kàn dào yī ge piào liang de gōng yuán zài páng biān， jiù zài shāng chǎng fù jìn， zhēn de hěn fāng biàn。 Wǒ yǐ jīng zhǎo dào xī mén le， dàn shì wǒ de péng yǒu hái méi dào。 Wǒ děng le jǐ fēn zhōng， zhōng yú kàn jiàn tā le。 Wǒ men jìn le shāng chǎng， guàng le hěn duō dì fang， mǎi le yī xiē dōng xi。 Xià wǔ wǒ men qù chī chuān cài， tè bié hǎo chī。 Huí jiā de shí hou， wǒ gěi lǎo bǎn fā le yī fēng yóu jiàn， gào su tā míng tiān wǒ yào qǐng jià。
▸ English Transcript
This morning, I called my friend and asked if she had time to go out for a stroll together. She said great, let's meet at the mall's west gate. I have a terrible sense of direction and often get lost, so I left early. On the way to the west gate, I saw a beautiful park next to me — right near the mall, really convenient. I had already found the west gate, but my friend hadn't arrived yet. I waited a few minutes and finally spotted her. We went into the mall, browsed many places, and bought some things. In the afternoon we went to eat Sichuan cuisine — especially delicious. On the way home, I sent my boss an email telling him I would take a day off tomorrow.
