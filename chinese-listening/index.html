Exercise 1: 每天的运动 — My Daily Exercise Easy

Short narrative, single speaker. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 这个人每个礼拜去几次健身房？

▸ Pinyin
Zhè ge rén měi ge lǐ bài qù jǐ cì jiàn shēn fáng？
▸ answer
三次。
Sān cì。
Three times.

2. 这个人喜欢什么运动？

▸ Pinyin
Zhè ge rén xǐ huān shén me yùn dòng？
▸ answer
跑步和打篮球。
Pǎo bù hé dǎ lán qiú。
Running and playing basketball.

3. 早上这个人喝什么？

▸ Pinyin
Zǎo shang zhè ge rén hē shén me？
▸ answer
一杯美式咖啡。
Yī bēi měi shì kā fēi。
A cup of Americano coffee.

4. 运动以后这个人觉得怎么样？

▸ Pinyin
Yùn dòng yǐ hòu zhè ge rén jué de zěn me yàng？
▸ answer
觉得很开心。
Jué de hěn kāi xīn。
Feels very happy.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
我很喜欢运动。🔊 每个礼拜，我去健身房三次。🔊 我也喜欢跑步和打篮球。🔊 早上我常常喝一杯美式咖啡。🔊 我朋友说，喝美式很好。🔊 有时候我吃一点儿巧克力。🔊 运动以后，我觉得很开心。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Wǒ hěn xǐ huān yùn dòng。 Měi ge lǐ bài， wǒ qù jiàn shēn fáng sān cì。 Wǒ yě xǐ huān pǎo bù hé dǎ lán qiú。 Zǎo shang wǒ cháng cháng hē yī bēi měi shì kā fēi。 Wǒ péng you shuō， hē měi shì hěn hǎo。 Yǒu shí hou wǒ chī yī diǎn er qiǎo kè lì。 Yùn dòng yǐ hòu， wǒ jué de hěn kāi xīn。
▸ English Transcript
I really like exercise. Every week, I go to the gym three times. I also like running and playing basketball. In the morning, I often drink a cup of Americano coffee. My friend says drinking Americano is very good. Sometimes I eat a little chocolate. After exercising, I feel very happy.
Exercise 2: 周末做什么 — What to Do on the Weekend Medium

Two-speaker dialogue. Listen and answer the questions below.

🔊 Listen to full dialogue (2 voices)

1. B 周末想做什么？

▸ Pinyin
B Zhōu mò xiǎng zuò shén me？
▸ answer
想和朋友打篮球。
Xiǎng hé péng you dǎ lán qiú。
He wants to play basketball with friends.

2. 书店在哪儿？

▸ Pinyin
Shū diàn zài nǎr？
▸ answer
在地铁站对面。
Zài dì tiě zhàn duì miàn。
Across from the subway station.

3. A 常常去那家书店吗？

▸ Pinyin
A cháng cháng qù nà jiā shū diàn ma？
▸ answer
常常去。那家书店有很多书。
Cháng cháng qù。 Nà jiā shū diàn yǒu hěn duō shū。
Yes, often. That bookstore has many books.

4. 今天他们要做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Jīn tiān tā men yào zuò shén me？
▸ answer
先去书店，然后去吃中国菜。
Xiān qù shū diàn， rán hòu qù chī Zhōng guó cài。
First go to the bookstore, then go eat Chinese food.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
A: 周末你想做什么？🔊 B: 我想和朋友打篮球。你呢？🔊 A: 我想去商店买东西。我也想去书店看书。🔊 B: 听起来很好。书店在哪儿？🔊 A: 在地铁站对面。你以前知道这个书店吗？🔊 B: 我不知道。🔊 A: 那家书店有很多书，我常常去。🔊 B: 真好。我们今天先去书店，然后去吃中国菜，怎么样？🔊 A: 太好了！我最喜欢吃中国菜。🔊 B: 我们一起走吧。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
A: Zhōu mò nǐ xiǎng zuò shén me？ B: Wǒ xiǎng hé péng you dǎ lán qiú。 Nǐ ne？ A: Wǒ xiǎng qù shāng diàn mǎi dōng xi。 Wǒ yě xiǎng qù shū diàn kàn shū。 B: Tīng qǐ lái hěn hǎo。 Shū diàn zài nǎr？ A: Zài dì tiě zhàn duì miàn。 Nǐ yǐ qián zhī dào zhè ge shū diàn ma？ B: Wǒ bù zhī dào。 A: Nà jiā shū diàn yǒu hěn duō shū， wǒ cháng cháng qù。 B: Zhēn hǎo。 Wǒ men jīn tiān xiān qù shū diàn， rán hòu qù chī Zhōng guó cài， zěn me yàng？ A: Tài hǎo le！ Wǒ zuì xǐ huān chī Zhōng guó cài。 B: Wǒ men yī qǐ zǒu ba。
▸ English Transcript
A: What do you want to do this weekend? B: I want to play basketball with friends. How about you? A: I want to go to a store to buy things. I also want to go to a bookstore to read. B: That sounds great. Where is the bookstore? A: Across from the subway station. Did you know about this bookstore before? B: I didn't know. A: That bookstore has many books — I go often. B: Really nice. Today, let's first go to the bookstore, then eat Chinese food. How about it? A: Great! I like eating Chinese food the most. B: Let's go together.
Exercise 3: 去年和今年 — Last Year and This Year Harder

Longer narrative, single speaker. More complex grammar. Listen carefully.

🔊 Listen to full passage

1. 以前这个人喜欢运动吗？

▸ Pinyin
Yǐ qián zhè ge rén xǐ huān yùn dòng ma？
▸ answer
以前不太喜欢。
Yǐ qián bú tài xǐ huān。
Before, didn't really like it.

2. 这个人去年开始做什么？

▸ Pinyin
Zhè ge rén qù nián kāi shǐ zuò shén me？
▸ answer
开始去健身房和跑步。
Kāi shǐ qù jiàn shēn fáng hé pǎo bù。
Started going to the gym and running.

3. 这个人平时早上喝什么？

▸ Pinyin
Zhè ge rén píng shí zǎo shang hē shén me？
▸ answer
一杯美式咖啡。
Yī bēi měi shì kā fēi。
A cup of Americano coffee.

4. 昨天这个人为什么不开心？

▸ Pinyin
Zuó tiān zhè ge rén wèi shén me bù kāi xīn？
▸ answer
因为开会迟到了，老板对他不高兴。
Yīn wèi kāi huì chí dào le， lǎo bǎn duì tā bù gāo xìng。
Because he was late to a meeting, and the boss was unhappy with him.

5. 这个人现在喜欢运动吗？

▸ Pinyin
Zhè ge rén xiàn zài xǐ huān yùn dòng ma？
▸ answer
现在很喜欢运动。
Xiàn zài hěn xǐ huān yùn dòng。
Now he really likes exercise.
▸ Hanzi Transcript
以前，我不太喜欢运动。🔊 去年我开始去健身房，每个礼拜三次。🔊 我也开始跑步，刚开始很难。🔊 现在跑步以后，我觉得很开心。🔊 平时我每天早上喝一杯美式咖啡。🔊 我也喜欢唱歌和跳舞。🔊 上个礼拜，我和朋友一起去商店买衣服。🔊 我们也去书店看书。🔊 昨天我有点儿不开心，因为我开会迟到了。🔊 老板对我不高兴。🔊 但是今天我又去健身房，运动以后觉得好多了。🔊 我现在很喜欢运动。🔊
▸ Pinyin Transcript
Yǐ qián， wǒ bú tài xǐ huān yùn dòng。 Qù nián wǒ kāi shǐ qù jiàn shēn fáng， měi ge lǐ bài sān cì。 Wǒ yě kāi shǐ pǎo bù， gāng kāi shǐ hěn nán。 Xiàn zài pǎo bù yǐ hòu， wǒ jué de hěn kāi xīn。 Píng shí wǒ měi tiān zǎo shang hē yī bēi měi shì kā fēi。 Wǒ yě xǐ huān chàng gē hé tiào wǔ。 Shàng ge lǐ bài， wǒ hé péng you yī qǐ qù shāng diàn mǎi yī fu。 Wǒ men yě qù shū diàn kàn shū。 Zuó tiān wǒ yǒu diǎn er bù kāi xīn， yīn wèi wǒ kāi huì chí dào le。 Lǎo bǎn duì wǒ bù gāo xìng。 Dàn shì jīn tiān wǒ yòu qù jiàn shēn fáng， yùn dòng yǐ hòu jué de hǎo duō le。 Wǒ xiàn zài hěn xǐ huān yùn dòng。
▸ English Transcript
Before, I didn't really like exercise. Last year I started going to the gym, three times every week. I also started running. At first it was very hard. Now, after running, I feel very happy. I usually drink a cup of Americano coffee every morning. I also like to sing and dance. Last week, I went with friends to a store to buy clothes. We also went to the bookstore to read. Yesterday I was a little unhappy, because I was late to a meeting. My boss was unhappy with me. But today I went to the gym again. After exercising, I felt much better. Now I really like to exercise.
